## What is Hyper-Compressed Ocean?

The **Hyper-Compressed Ocean** is a unique fishing toy that allows you to summon a fishable water sphere anywhere in the open world.

### What It Does

- Creates a huge water sphere fishing pool that lasts **15 minutes**
- Cooldown: **60 minutes**
- Can be used anywhere in the open world (mountains, deserts, cities)
- Cannot be used in dungeons or raids
- Any fish from various regions of Azeroth can be caught
- Loot table matches the zone where you use it

### Why Get It?

- **Convenience** - Fish anywhere without finding water
- **Completionist** - Toy collection achievement
- **Fishing Dailies** - Complete fishing quests anywhere
- **Unique Items** - Catch previously unobtainable items
- **Fun Factor** - Create ocean spheres on mountaintops

#### Phase 2: Complete "Secret Fish and Where to Find Them"

## Phase 1: Secret Fish of Mechagon

### Prerequisites

- **Location Access** - Mechagon Island (Battle for Azeroth zone)
- **Fishing Skill** - Any level works, but higher is better
- **Patience** - Low catch rates (~1% per cast)

### Objective

Catch and deliver **10 secret fish** to **Danielle Anglers** at Toothy Shallows in Mechagon Island.

### The 10 Fish Required

| Fish Name | Location in Mechagon | Notes |
|-----------|---------------------|-------|
| **Bottom Feeding Stinkfish** | Toothy Shallows | Near the trainer |
| **Pond Hopping Springfish** | Junkwatt Depot area | Small ponds |
| **Mechanical Blowfish** | Western coast | Saltwater areas |
| **Sludge-fouled Carp** | Rustbolt/polluted areas | Toxic water |
| **Solarsprocket Barbel** | Central Mechagon | Rivers |
| **Bolted Steelhead** | Northern areas | Rivers and streams |
| **Shadowy Cave Eel** | Caves around Mechagon | Dark water |
| **Spitting Clownfish** | Coral Beach | Coastal areas |
| **Energized Lightning Cod** | Sparkweaver Point | Electrified water |
| **Tasty Steelfin** | Various locations | Can be caught anywhere |

### How It Works

1. Fish in Mechagon waters
2. Each fish has ~1% catch rate
3. When caught, you auto-accept a quest
4. Bring the fish to Danielle Anglers at Toothy Shallows
5. Turn in the quest for 25 Rustbolt Reputation
6. Repeat until you have all 10

### Farming Tips

- **Use fishing pools** - Higher catch rates
- **Bring Lures** - Increase catch chance
- **Fish while waiting** - Do this during queue times
- **Multiple days** - Don't burn out, take breaks
- **Use Fishing Chair** - More comfortable AFK fishing

### Reputation Bonus

Each fish turned in grants **25 Rustbolt Reputation**, and you can turn in one fish per day for continued rep after completing the achievement.

### Reward

- **Achievement**: Secret Fish of Mechagon
- **Item**: Secret Fish Goggles

## Phase 2: Secret Fish and Where to Find Them

This is the main achievement that rewards the Hyper-Compressed Ocean toy.

### Prerequisites

- **Secret Fish Goggles** (from Phase 1)
- Access to various zones across Azeroth
- Significantly more patience

### How the Goggles Work

1. Equip the Secret Fish Goggles
2. A tiny puffer fish spawns within **40 yards** every **30 seconds**
3. Click the fish before it despawns (~5-10 seconds)
4. You have a **low chance** to receive a rare secret fish
5. Repeat until you collect all required fish

### The Fish You Need

There are approximately **30 rare secret fish** divided into categories.

### Time-Specific Fish (2 Total)

Must be caught during specific times of day.

#### Elusive Moonfish
- **Time**: Night only
- **Safe Window**: 12:00 AM / 00:00 server time
- **Location**: Anywhere
- **Tip**: Start fishing at midnight to guarantee night time

#### Golden Sunsoaker
- **Time**: Daytime only
- **Safe Window**: Daily reset time (varies by region)
- **Location**: Anywhere
- **Tip**: Fish during midday to be safe

---

- **Location**: Bottom of the lake under Nordrassil
- **Notes**: Underwater location
- **Bug Warning**: Approach on foot, don't fly in directly

### Dead-Only Fish

Some fish can **only be caught while you're dead**.

**How to Do This:**
1. Die near the location
2. While dead (ghost form), equip goggles
3. Fish will spawn as a ghost
4. Click them to collect
5. Resurrect after collecting

**Fish That May Require This:**
- Specific fish in certain zones (community reports vary)
- Test both alive and dead if you're having trouble

## Farming Strategy

### Optimal Route

**Week 1: Universal Fish**
- Farm in Nagrand (Outland) for 2-3 hours
- Should get most/all universal fish

**Week 2: Time-Specific Fish**
- Set alarms for midnight (Elusive Moonfish)
- Fish during midday (Golden Sunsoaker)

**Week 3-4: Zone-Specific Fish**
- Create a route hitting all zones
- Use portal rooms for fast travel
- Check off fish as you get them

**Week 5+: Finish Stragglers**
- Revisit zones you're missing
- Check if any require "dead" status
- Consult updated guides for bug fixes

### Expected Timeline

- **Fast (Lucky)**: 10-15 hours spread over 2-3 weeks
- **Average**: 20-30 hours spread over 4-6 weeks
- **Unlucky**: 40+ hours spread over 2-3 months

### Drop Rates

- Rare secret fish have **extremely low drop rates** (estimated 1-5%)
- Each goggles spawn gives you one chance
- Spawns occur every 30 seconds
- You'll catch hundreds of common fish before completing

## Common Problems & Solutions

### Problem: Fish Not Spawning

**Solution:**
- Make sure goggles are equipped
- Check you're in the correct zone for zone-specific fish
- Wait the full 30 seconds between spawns
- Try relogging

### Problem: Can't Find Specific Location

**Solution:**
- Use HandyNotes addon
- Check Wowhead comments for updated coordinates
- Ask in general chat for help
- Watch YouTube video guides

### Problem: Already Have Some Fish But Don't Know Which

**Solution:**
- Install HandyNotes: Secret Fish addon
- It tracks automatically which you've collected
- Cross-reference with achievement tracker

### Problem: Chromie Not Appearing

**Solution:**
- Chromie is a daily visitor (not always present)
- Check back each day
- She appears at coordinates 70, 31 in Mechagon

### Problem: Fish Bugged in Certain Zones

**Solution:**
- Try approaching on foot instead of flying
- Try while alive vs. dead
- Check Wowhead comments for recent workarounds
- May need to wait for hotfix

## Using the Toy

### How to Use

1. Open Toy Box (Shift+P → Collections → Toys tab)
2. Search for "Hyper-Compressed Ocean"
3. Click to use (creates water sphere)
4. Fish from the sphere for 15 minutes
5. 60-minute cooldown before next use

### Best Uses

**Fishing Dailies**
- Complete "catch X fish" quests anywhere
- No need to travel to water

**Convenience**
- Fish while waiting for raid/M+ groups
- Fish in cities, on mountains, in deserts

**Unique Catches**
- Loot table based on your current zone
- Can catch zone-specific fish anywhere
- Some previously unobtainable items can drop

**Achievement Hunting**
- Some fishing achievements easier with this toy
- Can fish in convenient locations

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I buy this on the Auction House?**
A: No, it's a toy bound to your account. Must complete the achievement.

**Q: Does fishing skill matter?**
A: Higher fishing skill helps with Phase 1 (Mechagon fish). For goggles fish, skill doesn't affect spawn rate, but may affect catch success.

**Q: Can I do this on multiple characters?**
A: The achievement is account-wide once completed. You only need to do it once.

**Q: How long does this really take?**
A: Highly variable. Some players report 15-20 hours, others 40+ hours. RNG is a major factor.

**Q: Are there any fishing requirements?**
A: No minimum fishing skill required, but higher skill makes it easier.

**Q: Can I do this while leveling?**
A: Yes, but you need access to Mechagon (BFA content) and various old zones.

**Q: Is it worth it?**
A: For completionists and fishing enthusiasts, yes. For practical use, somewhat limited but convenient.

**Q: Can I use it in Mythic+/Raids?**
A: No, only works in open-world zones.
