## Beast Mastery Hunter

Beast Mastery is the most mobile Hunter specialization, allowing you to cast all abilities while moving. Excels at sustained single-target and cleave damage with strong pet management.

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- 100% mobility - all abilities castable while moving
- Strong sustained single-target damage
- Excellent cleave with Beast Cleave
- Great for learning encounters
- Pet utility and damage

**Weaknesses:**
- Lower burst damage compared to other specs
- Pet management required
- Limited AoE burst options

## Marksmanship Hunter

Marksmanship is the precision-based Hunter specialization focused on powerful aimed shots and burst damage windows. Excels in coordinated burst and execute phases.

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- Highest burst damage among Hunter specs
- Strong execute phase with Bullseye
- Excellent in coordinated burst windows
- Long-range advantage
- No pet dependency (Lone Wolf)

**Weaknesses:**
- Requires planning for burst windows
- Cast times reduce mobility
- Vulnerability during Aimed Shot casts
- Lower sustained damage outside cooldowns

## Survival Hunter

Survival is the melee Hunter specialization focused on bleeding effects, traps, and tactical gameplay. Excels in Mythic+ with strong AoE burst and sustained damage.

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- Excellent sustained AoE and cleave
- Strong utility with traps and crowd control
- High mobility with melee range
- Great self-healing with Exhilaration
- Best Mythic+ Hunter spec

**Weaknesses:**
- Melee range increases danger
- Complex rotation with DoT tracking
- Lower burst compared to ranged specs
- Requires target swapping management

## General Hunter Tips

### Utility Abilities

- **Feign Death** - Drop combat, avoid mechanics, reset threat
- **Misdirection** - Threat management for tank
- **Freezing Trap** - Crowd control (PvP and dungeons)
- **Tar Trap** - Slow enemies in an area
- **Flare** - Reveal stealth and invisible enemies
- **Aspect of the Cheetah** - Group movement speed

### Defensive Cooldowns

- **Exhilaration** - Large instant heal (30% HP)
- **Survival of the Fittest** - 30% damage reduction for 6 seconds
- **Aspect of the Turtle** - Immunity to damage for 8 seconds (cannot attack)
