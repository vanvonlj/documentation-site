# Death Knight Class Guide

Comprehensive guide for Blood Death Knight tanking in World of Warcraft: Midnight (Season 1, Patch 12.0).

## Class Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Role** | Tank |
| **Armor Type** | Plate |
| **Resource** | Runes / Runic Power |
| **Hero Talents** | San'layn, Deathbringer |
| **Best for M+** | San'layn (self-sustain) / Deathbringer (damage) |
| **Best for Raid** | Deathbringer (ST damage) / San'layn (magic-heavy fights) |

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- Exceptional self-healing via Death Strike
- Strong magic damage mitigation with Anti-Magic Shell
- Powerful AoE grip (Gorefiend's Grasp) for Mythic+
- Great sustained damage for a tank
- Reactive playstyle rewards skilled players

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited mobility (no dash or leap)
- Reactive rather than proactive mitigation
- Poor against sustained ticking damage without cooldowns
- Requires strong resource management
- Bone Shield upkeep can feel punishing if neglected

## General Blood DK Tips

### Defensive Abilities

- **Death Strike** — Your bread and butter; heals based on recent damage taken. Time it AFTER big hits, not before
- **Vampiric Blood** — 30% max HP increase + 30% healing received for 10 sec. Use frequently, not just emergencies
- **Anti-Magic Shell** — Absorbs magic damage and converts to Runic Power. Essential for magic-heavy fights
- **Icebound Fortitude** — 30% damage reduction. Emergency button, avoid overlapping with Vampiric Blood
- **Bone Shield (via Marrowrend)** — Passive mitigation floor. Never let it fall off

### Mythic+ Tips

- Open pulls with **Gorefiend's Grasp** to stack mobs, then drop **Death and Decay** on the pile
- Use **Death Strike** reactively after damage spikes, not proactively
- Keep enough Runic Power banked (50+) so you're never caught with an empty bar during a spike
- **Anti-Magic Shell** is your best friend on magic-heavy packs and affixes
- Manage **Bone Shield** proactively — Marrowrend at 5 stacks or below, not at 0

### Raid Tanking Tips

- Coordinate tank swaps around your cooldown availability
- Time **Death Strike** against predictable tank buster abilities
- Use **Anti-Magic Shell** to soak mechanics that would otherwise require a healer external
- **Consumption** empowered to higher ranks is a strong raid cooldown in dangerous moments
- Communicate your **Vampiric Blood** usage with healers for efficient external timing
