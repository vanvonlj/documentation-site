# Leveling Routes

Practical "what dungeon do I spam right now" reference. Assumes **you queue tank** (instant queues) and **collect every dungeon quest** in the relevant area before walking in — dungeon quests are routinely **30-50% of the XP** for that level bracket.

## How to actually level fast on Chromie Craft

ChromieCraft's **base XP rate is x2 through level 80** (verified on the [Rates & Changes page](https://www.chromiecraft.com/en/rates-n-changes/)). This makes dungeon spam already comfortable; combine it with the techniques below and you'll move fast.

1. **Queue tank, every time.** Instant pop, every pop. DPS/heal queues at level brackets above 30 are 5-15 minutes; tank queues are under 1 minute.
2. **Grab every dungeon quest before queueing.** The quest hubs are usually 1-2 hops from the dungeon entrance (or in the city). Use the **map** of the relevant zone — yellow exclamation marks near dungeon portals are dungeon quests.
3. **First dungeon of the day = bonus XP** via the **Random Dungeon Finder daily** (2-3x normal XP for first random pop per character per day).
4. **Heirlooms.** Shoulders + chest = +20% XP combined. **Get them at level 1** if you have any 80 alt; otherwise farm them with a friend.
5. **Joyous Journeys** boost — if it's active (currently +50% XP through May 20, 2026), you're already at effective **x3 XP** without doing anything extra.
6. **Recruit-A-Friend** — new accounts get permanent rested XP (kills/quests doubled) for 30 days or until 68. Pair with a friend or alt account to lock this in.
7. **Rest XP.** Log out in an inn between sessions. Doubles XP from kills until consumed.
8. **Queue from inside a dungeon** — when one ends, queue the next *immediately*. Hearthstone if your group disbands; don't waste the cooldown.

**Want to slow down?** Use `.weekendxp rate 1` to drop to blizzlike pacing, or `.xp off` to freeze your level entirely (free, toggleable any time).

**Want a permanent twink without abandoning your main?** Use `.carboncopy` — it duplicates your character, so you can lock the copy at level 19/29/39/etc. and keep leveling the original. This is a Chromie Craft-only command and the cleanest way to maintain bracket twinks.

## Heirloom priority for warriors

**Earnable from Argent Tournament / Wintergrasp / Stone Keeper's Shards**:

| Slot | Item | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **Shoulders** | Polished Spaulders of Valor | +10% XP, plate, str/sta |
| **Chest** | Polished Breastplate of Valor | +10% XP, plate, str/sta |
| **2H weapon** | Bloodied Arcanite Reaper | Best leveling 2H (huge weapon damage) |
| **1H weapon** | Venerable Mass of McGowan | Tank 1H mace |
| **Trinket** | Discerning Eye of the Beast | +44 spell power... skip for warriors |
| **Trinket** | Tattered Dreadmist Mantle | Cloth, skip |

For warrior leveling: **Shoulders + Chest + 2H Reaper** is the holy trinity. The Reaper carries you from 1 to 80 and never falls off until ~level 70 raid weapons.

## Level brackets

### 13–20: First taste

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Ragefire Chasm** | 13–17 | Horde-flavored, in Orgrimmar. Two quests in Org. |
| **Wailing Caverns** | 17–22 | The Barrens. **Many quests** (Discipulus quest chain, Glowing Shard, Druids of the Fang). Spam this if you can get in. |
| **The Deadmines** | 17–22 | Westfall (Horde via RDF). Quest is in Sentinel Hill (Alliance) — Horde can't easily collect. |
| **Shadowfang Keep** | 18–22 | Silverpine. Couple of quests, quick run. |

**Recommended:** **WC → SFK → WC** loop until 22. WC has the best quest density for the level.

### 20–30: Wing it through SM

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Blackfathom Deeps** | 20–25 | Ashenvale. Pre-pull the fire braziers quest. |
| **The Stockades** | 22–28 | Stormwind (Horde via RDF). Tiny. Easy emblems. |
| **Gnomeregan** | 24–30 | Long. **Skip unless you have a fast group.** Or do it once for the achievement and **Manual Crowd Pummeler** for your DPS off-spec. |
| **Razorfen Kraul** | 25–30 | Quick. Three quests (Charge of the Quilboar, Brutal Armament, Trolls of the Barrens). |

**Recommended:** **BFD twice → RFK → SM Graveyard** as you hit 26.

### 26–40: Scarlet Monastery shuffle

This is the SM era. **All four wings** drop quest XP from the Scarlet Monastery Doan/Mograine quest chains.

| Wing | Best at | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **SM: Graveyard** | 26–32 | Tirisfal. Quick farm; **Death Knight transmog set** drops from Bloodmage Thalnos. |
| **SM: Library** | 30–36 | Doan drops **Hypnotic Blade** (Arms-viable 1H). |
| **SM: Armory** | 32–38 | **Herod's Shoulder** (BiS bracket plate shoulders) + **Ravager** (BiS leveling 2H polearm — you want this for Arms even if you have the Reaper heirloom; it has +50 stats). |
| **SM: Cathedral** | 36–42 | Mograine + Whitemane. **Mograine's Might** (BiS bracket 2H sword). |

**Recommended:** **SM Library → SM Armory → SM Cathedral** chain. If you get Armory in queue twice, great — Ravager is worth re-runs.

### 38–50: The mid-leveling slog

Where most leveling slows down. Few dungeons, longer travel, and quest density drops.

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Razorfen Downs** | 38–45 | Southern Barrens. Quick, decent quests. |
| **Uldaman** | 40–48 | Long. **Worth it for Stoneslayer / Hammer of the Northern Wind**. |
| **Maraudon (any wing)** | 42–50 | Three wings — Princess wing is the most-run. Long. **Princess drops good leveling plate.** |
| **Zul'Farrak** | 44–52 | The pyramid event is a chunk of XP. **Sul'thraze the Lasher** (BoP 2H sword via two trash drops) is iconic. |

**Recommended:** **Maraudon Princess → ZF → ZF**. Skip Uldaman unless you're hunting specific drops or bored of Maraudon.

### 50–60: BRD, the cathedral of dungeons

Once you're 50, the queue becomes: **Sunken Temple → BRD → BRD**. BRD has so many quests you can take **a 5-quest chunk** to it twice and complete different objectives.

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Sunken Temple** | 50–56 | Long, mazey. Quests in Swamp of Sorrows + Hinterlands. |
| **Blackrock Depths** | 52–60 | Pick up: Reign of Thaurissan, Shadowforge Key, Plugger Spazzring's bar quests (drink event). **The longest single dungeon** — usually run as targeted segments (Bar wing only, Emperor wing only). |
| **Lower Blackrock Spire** | 55–60 | Long. Drops many BoEs. |
| **Dire Maul (any wing)** | 56–60 | DM-North tribute run is the best XP (large named pulls, side rooms skipped). |
| **Stratholme Live** | 58–60 | Quick if you skip houses. |
| **Stratholme UD** | 58–60 | The 45-min Baron run gives a unique quest reward + Eastern Plaguelands rep. |
| **Scholomance** | 58–60 | Big, dense. Quests in WPL. |

**Recommended:** Pick up **all BRD quests** in Searing Gorge / Blackrock Mountain / Ironforge / Orgrimmar before your first BRD run, then chain **2 BRD partial runs → DM-North tribute → Strat UD** to reach 60.

### 58–68: Hellfire to early Outland

You **enter Outland at 58** (through the Dark Portal in Blasted Lands). Outland quests give *significantly* more XP than Eastern Plaguelands quests, so **stop dungeon spam temporarily** at 58 to do **Hellfire Peninsula quests** until 60-62, then resume dungeons.

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Hellfire Ramparts** | 58–63 | First Outland dungeon. Quick. |
| **The Blood Furnace** | 60–66 | Hellfire Citadel chain quests. Mind-control mechanics make it interesting. |
| **The Slave Pens** | 60–66 | Cenarion Expedition rep. |
| **The Underbog** | 61–67 | Bigger, slower. |
| **Mana-Tombs** | 62–68 | Auchindoun — quick. |

**Recommended:** **Hellfire Ramparts → Blood Furnace → Slave Pens** loop until 65. Hellfire dungeons give the most XP per minute in this bracket.

### 65–72: Outland endgame + first Northrend

By 65 you can start tackling the harder TBC dungeons. **At 68 you can enter Northrend** — go to **Borean Tundra** or **Howling Fjord** and switch to Northrend questing for several levels of "fresh" quest XP, then resume Northrend dungeons at 70.

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Auchenai Crypts** | 63–69 | Quick. |
| **Sethekk Halls** | 65–71 | Quick. |
| **Steamvault** | 66–72 | Ratings reasonable. |
| **Shattered Halls** | 67–72 | Hardest. Long. |
| **Old Hillsbrad** | 66–71 | Long event-based; good XP but slow per-pop. |
| **Black Morass** | 68–72 | Wave defense, fast XP. **Run this** at 68-70 if you can get a group. |
| **Mechanar** | 68–72 | Tempest Keep — quick. |
| **Botanica** | 67–72 | Tempest Keep. |
| **Arcatraz** | 69–72 | Hardest TK; longer. |
| **Magisters' Terrace** | 68–72 | Quick. **Drops better-than-bracket loot** thanks to being a 3.0 dungeon. |

**Recommended:** Outland-side: **Mechanar → Black Morass → Mechanar**. Northrend-side at 68: **Utgarde Keep → The Nexus**.

### 68–80: Northrend grind

Northrend dungeons all give very competitive XP. **Spam whatever pops** in your random dungeon queue.

| Dungeon | Best at | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Utgarde Keep** | 68–72 | First Northrend dungeon. Quick. |
| **The Nexus** | 69–73 | Decent length. |
| **Azjol-Nerub** | 72–74 | Short, three bosses. |
| **Ahn'kahet** | 72–75 | Volazj is interesting. |
| **Drak'Tharon Keep** | 73–76 | Quick. |
| **The Violet Hold** | 73–76 | **Wave defense — fastest XP/min** of any dungeon in the bracket. |
| **Gundrak** | 76–79 | Quick. |
| **Halls of Stone** | 76–79 | Tribunal event slows it down. |
| **Halls of Lightning** | 77–80 | Loken is a wipe risk. Solid XP. |
| **The Oculus** | 76–79 | **Most groups leave on sight.** Drake mechanics confuse pugs. If you actually run it, fast and decent loot. |
| **Utgarde Pinnacle** | 78–80 | Skadi air phase + Ymiron. Solid. |
| **Culling of Stratholme** | 78–80 | Long event-based. Bronze drake on timed run. |

**Recommended:** **Violet Hold every random pop you can chase**. Otherwise just queue random.

## Estimated time-to-80

With heirlooms (shoulders + chest + 2H reaper), tank queues, and proper quest pickup, at **ChromieCraft's default x2 XP rate**:

- **1 → 60:** ~25-35 hours.
- **60 → 70:** ~12-18 hours.
- **70 → 80:** ~15-20 hours.

**Total:** ~55-75 hours of /played at base x2 rate. With Joyous Journeys (+50%) currently active, expect closer to **35-50 hours**. With RAF rested XP layered on top, faster still.

If you skip dungeon quests and just queue random repeatedly, **add 50%+** to the times above. Quests are not optional for fast leveling.

If you're running a **challenge mode** (Hardcore / Ironman / Bloodthirsty), rates revert to **x1 blizzlike** — roughly double the base x2 estimate. The leveling experience becomes a much different (and more deliberate) game.

## After 80: gearing for heroics

The moment you hit 80, your priority is **getting to Defense 540** for heroics. Quickest path:

1. Buy **crafted Saronite gear** (Tempered Saronite Helm/Chestpiece/Legplates etc.) — relatively cheap on the AH at 80.
2. Run **regular Northrend dungeons** until you have 5-6 blue tank pieces.
3. Cap Defense via **rep gear** (Argent Crusade, Wyrmrest Accord, Knights of the Ebon Blade) and **Triumph emblem ToC** quests.
4. Step into **heroics**, then chase Frost Emblems via the random heroic daily — see the [WotLK dungeons](./wotlk-dungeons#strategy-which-dungeons-to-farm-at-80) page for the BiS list.
