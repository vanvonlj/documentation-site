# Talent Build (Arms 54/17/0)

The standard PvE Arms build for WotLK 3.3.5a — works for everything from level 30 questing through ICC heroic 5-mans. Talent reset is **free** on Chromie Craft, so respec freely as you level into more talents.

> **Capstone:** [![Bladestorm](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/large/ability_warrior_bladestorm.jpg) Bladestorm](https://www.wowhead.com/wotlk/spell=46924) — unlocks at **level 60** (51 talent points). Your single biggest power spike as Arms.

**Open the live build:** [Wowhead Talent Calculator (Warrior)](https://www.wowhead.com/wotlk/talent-calc/warrior) — drop in the points below.

## Distribution

- **Arms:** 54 points
- **Fury:** 17 points
- **Protection:** 0 points

## Stat priority (level 80)

```
Hit (8% / 263) → Expertise (26 cap) → Strength → ArmorPen → Crit → Haste → AP
```

- **Hit** to **8%** for melee specials. **9%** if you're not in a Draenei group (Heroic Presence raid buff covers 1%).
- **Expertise** to **26** to eliminate dodges from bosses.
- **Strength** scales linearly with damage — biggest stat after caps.
- **Armor Penetration** scales hardest above ~50% gear quality. Above the 100% APen cap, swap to crit.
- **Crit** stays valuable due to Wrecking Crew, Sudden Death, and Deep Wounds procs.
- **Haste** is the weakest melee stat at low gear levels; rises with ICC ilvl.
