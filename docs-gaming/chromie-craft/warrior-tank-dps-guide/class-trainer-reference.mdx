# Class Trainer Reference

Every warrior ability in WotLK 3.3.5a, in the order you actually learn them. Use this as a checklist when you ding — visit your trainer, learn anything new, set hotkeys.

> **All abilities below are taught by warrior trainers in capital cities.** Your trainer is in **Orgrimmar (Valley of Honor)** for Tauren — Sergra Darkthorn, Hanashi, etc. Druids visit Thunder Bluff trainers; warriors don't have a Thunder Bluff option.

## Stances

You have three stances. **You learn one per level milestone**, and most abilities are gated to a specific stance.

| Level | Stance | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **1** | **Battle Stance** | +5% damage, +10% crit damage, +1 rage/sec out of combat. Default leveling stance. |
| **10** | **Defensive Stance** | -10% damage taken, +10% threat, -10% damage dealt. **Tank stance**. |
| **30** | **Berserker Stance** | +10% physical damage taken, +3% crit, +10% melee/ranged crit chance. Fury DPS stance. |

**Stance-locked abilities:**
- Battle: Charge, Overpower, Retaliation, Mocking Blow, Thunder Clap (and most generic stuff)
- Defensive: Shield Block, Shield Slam, Revenge, Disarm, Taunt, Spell Reflection, Last Stand, Shield Wall, Devastate, Concussion Blow, Shockwave
- Berserker: Whirlwind, Berserker Rage, Intercept, Recklessness, Pummel

> **Glyph of Stances** does not exist in 3.3.5a. You can't reduce stance-swap rage cost beyond the base 5 lost rage (Tactical Mastery talent in Arms tree reduces this — most builds dump 5/5 in Tactical Mastery).

## Base abilities by level

### Level 1–10 (Battle Stance only)

| Level | Ability | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| 1 | **Battle Shout** | 1-min party buff | +AP. Refresh every ~2 minutes. Always on. |
| 1 | **Heroic Strike** | Next-melee replacer | Rage dump. Doesn't cost a GCD. |
| 1 | Strike (auto-attack) | Passive | Just turn it on. |
| 4 | **Charge** | Battle stance, OOC | Stuns target 1.5s, generates 15 rage. **Open every pull.** |
| 4 | **Rend** | DoT | Bleed. Decent leveling filler. Not used at 80. |
| 6 | **Thunder Clap** | Battle (later all) | AoE damage + slow. **Mandatory for tanking AoE.** |
| 8 | **Hamstring** | Battle/Berserker | -50% movement. Snare. |
| 10 | **Defensive Stance** | Stance | Unlocks tank stance. |
| 10 | **Sunder Armor** | Defensive | Tank threat + armor reduction. **Devastate replaces this** later. |
| 10 | **Bloodrage** | All stances | Free 10 rage instantly + over time. **Pre-pull staple.** |

### Level 11–20 (more tools)

| Level | Ability | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| 12 | **Taunt** | Defensive only. Forces the target to attack you for 3 seconds. **Always have hotkeyed.** |
| 14 | **Demoralizing Shout** | -AP debuff on enemies in 10yd. Mitigation tool — keep it up on bosses. |
| 14 | **Revenge** | Defensive. Strong threat ability that requires a parry/block/dodge to be available. **Free threat.** |
| 14 | **Shield Bash** | Battle/Defensive. Interrupt + silence (target school). 12-second CD. |
| 16 | **Shield Block** | Defensive only. +75% block chance, +100% block value, 10 sec / 30 sec CD (talented to 60s/+10% block). **Use on every pull as tank.** |
| 18 | **Cleave** | Next-melee replacer. AoE rage dump (2 targets, 3 with glyph). **Use instead of Heroic Strike** during AoE pulls. |
| 18 | **Disarm** | Defensive. Disarms target's weapon for 10s. Useful on caster-with-staff or hard-hitting melee bosses. |
| 18 | **Mocking Blow** | Battle. Forces target to attack you for 6 sec. **Long-range backup taunt** for Defensive's Taunt cooldown. |
| 20 | **Slam** | Any stance. 1.5s cast, big damage. Arms filler ability. Not used by Prot. |

### Level 21–30 (Berserker stance unlock, real tanking)

| Level | Ability | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| 22 | **Challenging Shout** | Defensive. **AoE taunt** (10 yd, 6 sec). 10-min cooldown. **Emergency button** when adds break loose. |
| 24 | **Heroic Throw** | Defensive (taught at 50, actually — see correction below). |
| 24 | **Recklessness** | Berserker. +20% crit, 12s / 5min CD. Open with this on bosses for DPS. |
| 28 | **Shield Wall** | Defensive. -60% damage taken, 12s. **The big tank cooldown.** Glyph drops effect to -40% but cuts CD to 2 min — usually worth it for 5-mans. |
| 30 | **Berserker Stance** | Stance. Unlocks Berserker abilities. |
| 30 | **Intercept** | Berserker, in combat. Charge equivalent for combat — gap-closer. Generates rage from a stun. |
| 30 | **Retaliation** | Battle. 12s of auto-counterattacks on melee hits. 30 min CD — once-per-dungeon button. Decent solo tool. |

### Level 31–50 (talented abilities show up)

By level 30 you've unlocked **21-point talents** in your tree. The big three:

| Talent | Tree | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Mortal Strike** | Arms (21pt) | 4s CD, primary Arms ability. -25% healing taken on target. |
| **Bloodthirst** | Fury (21pt) | 4s CD, primary Fury ability. Heals you for 1% of max HP. |
| **Shield Slam** | Prot (21pt) | 6s CD, **the highest threat-per-rage ability**. Dispels one magic effect. **Mandatory for any tank.** |

| Level | Ability | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| 32 | **Berserker Rage** | Berserker. Removes fear/sap/incapacitate, immune for 10s. **Press on Bronjahm, Ingvar, Loken, etc.** |
| 36 | **Whirlwind** | Berserker. AoE in 8yd. Fury core ability. **8s CD with glyph.** |
| 38 | **Pummel** | Berserker. Interrupt — silence school for 4s. **Lower CD than Shield Bash, doesn't require shield.** Fury/Arms interrupt. |
| 42 | **Execute** | Battle/Berserker. Sub-20% finisher. **Massive damage.** |
| 50 | **Heroic Throw** | All stances. Ranged threat ability. **Pull tool** at 30yd. Useful for grabbing skipped mobs. |
| 50 | **Intervene** | Battle. Run to ally, take next physical hit for them. Breaks stuns/snares on you. **Save the healer** tool. |
| 50 | **Spell Reflection** | Defensive. Reflects next spell for 5s, 10s CD. **Massive vs. Magisters' Terrace, Ahn'kahet, HoR Phantom Mages.** |
| 60 | **Commanding Shout** | All stances. +HP party buff, replaces Battle Shout for tanks. |

### Level 60+ (51-point capstones)

At **level 60** you unlock your **51-point talent** — the spec-defining capstone:

| Talent | Tree | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Bladestorm** | Arms (51pt) | 6 sec channeled AoE, immune to most disables. **Best Arms button**. 1.5 min CD (1.15 with glyph). |
| **Shockwave** | Prot (51pt) | Cone stun + damage. 4s stun, 20s CD. **Tank's interrupt + control button.** Threat is decent too. |
| **Titan's Grip** | Fury (51pt) | Passive — wield 2H weapons in both hands. **Single biggest Fury power spike.** |

### Level 65–80 (talents fill out)

After level 60, you're gaining 1 talent point per level — **filling out off-tree synergies and key talents** rather than learning new spells. The big 3.0+ talented abilities:

| Talent | Tree | Available | Notes |
|--------|------|-----------|-------|
| **Devastate** | Prot (Tier 5, 4-pt) | ~level 30 | **Replaces Sunder Armor** as your filler. Hits for damage AND applies a stack. |
| **Concussion Blow** | Prot (Tier 7, 1-pt) | ~level 40 | 5s stun, 30s CD. **Bonus interrupt.** |
| **Vigilance** | Prot (Tier 8, 1-pt) | ~level 45 | Buff on a friendly target — they transfer 10% threat to you, and your taunt CD is reset when they're hit. **Cast once at start of dungeon.** |
| **Sweeping Strikes** | Arms (Tier 4, 1-pt) | ~level 25 | 10s of cleaves on every melee swing. **Massive AoE for Arms.** |
| **Sudden Death** | Arms (Tier 8, 3-pt) | ~level 45 | Procs free Execute when you land melee. Core Arms damage. |
| **Heroic Fury** | Arms or Fury | mid-tier | Removes immobilize + resets Intercept CD. Mobility tool. |

## What changes when

### Major rotation milestones for tanking

- **Level 4:** Charge unlocks — every pull starts with it.
- **Level 10:** Defensive Stance + Sunder Armor + Bloodrage. **Tank kit begins.**
- **Level 16:** Shield Block. Critical mitigation cooldown.
- **Level 28:** Shield Wall. Your "oh shit" button.
- **Level 30:** Shield Slam (talented Prot). The threat ability.
- **Level 30:** Devastate (talented Prot). Filler ability — finally, threat that does damage.
- **Level 50:** Spell Reflection. Massive vs. caster bosses.
- **Level 60:** Shockwave. Cone stun + threat = AoE pull god mode.
- **Level 80:** Endgame rotation locked in.

### Major rotation milestones for DPS (Arms)

- **Level 4:** Charge.
- **Level 20:** Sweeping Strikes (talented at level 25 or so when Arms tree is deep enough).
- **Level 30:** Mortal Strike (21pt talent). **Arms identity.**
- **Level 42:** Execute. Sub-20% finisher.
- **Level 60:** Bladestorm. Capstone.
- **Level 80:** Sudden Death procs + Bladestorm rotation locked in.

### Major rotation milestones for DPS (Fury)

- **Level 4:** Charge.
- **Level 30:** Bloodthirst. Fury identity.
- **Level 36:** Whirlwind. AoE.
- **Level 60:** Titan's Grip. **2H dual-wield unlocks.** This is when Fury becomes Fury.
- **Level 80:** Bloodsurge procs (Slam instant) keep the rotation flowing.

## Talent point milestones

You learn **1 talent point per level starting at level 10**.

| Total points | Level reached | Tree depth unlocked |
|--------------|---------------|---------------------|
| 1 | 10 | Tier 1 |
| 5 | 14 | Tier 2 |
| 10 | 19 | Tier 3 |
| 15 | 24 | Tier 4 |
| 20 | 29 | Tier 5 |
| 25 | 34 | Tier 6 |
| 30 | 39 | Tier 7 |
| 35 | 44 | Tier 8 |
| 40 | 49 | Tier 9 |
| 45 | 54 | Tier 10 |
| 50 | 59 | Tier 11 (one tier from capstone) |
| 51 | 60 | **51-point capstone** (Bladestorm / Shockwave / Titan's Grip) |
| 71 | 80 | Full talent build |

**Dual Talents** unlock at **level 40**, costing **1000g** (or 100g on Chromie Craft if reduced — check the in-game vendor). After buying, you get a second talent loadout you can swap to **anywhere out of combat** with no respec cost. Tank spec + DPS spec is the standard pairing.
