# WotLK Dungeons (68–80)

Northrend 5-mans. The design philosophy is **less CC, more boss mechanics**. Most pulls can be tank-and-spank with one or two interrupts. Bosses lean on positioning, target switching, and avoidable damage instead of "polymorph the warlock or wipe."

## Heroic gating

- **Level 80** required.
- **Honored** with the dungeon's faction to buy the heroic key/badge for the level 70 dungeon equivalents — this doesn't apply to **Northrend** dungeons. WotLK heroics are unlocked **automatically at 80**, no key required, just queue Heroic via RDF.
- **Trial of the Champion**, **Forge of Souls**, **Pit of Saron**, and **Halls of Reflection** are the post-launch 5-mans (patch 3.2 / 3.3) — drop higher item-level loot.

## Borean Tundra & Howling Fjord — first stop

### Utgarde Keep (UK)

- **Level range:** 68–72 — Howling Fjord. Your first Northrend dungeon.
- **Bosses:** Prince Keleseth, Skarvald & Dalronn, Ingvar the Plunderer.

Smooth opener. Mostly tank-and-spank.

- **Tank:** **Keleseth** ice-traps a random player and spawns 3 skeletons — peel them off the trapped player. **Skarvald & Dalronn** is a **two-boss simultaneously** fight; one ghost, one physical, both alive until both dead. Tank both, group cleaves. **Ingvar** has two phases — humanoid and undead (he revives) — different fear/stun mechanics; Berserker Rage breaks fear.
- **DPS:** Cleave both bosses on Skarvald + Dalronn. Bladestorm during Ingvar's add wave between phases.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Plate Helm of the Great Wyrm** (Ingvar HC — solid pre-raid tank helm), **Royal Crest of Lordaeron** (Keleseth HC — caster), **Annhylde's Ring** (Ingvar HC — caster ring).

### The Nexus (Nex)

- **Level range:** 69–72 — Borean Tundra (Coldarra).
- **Bosses:** Grand Magus Telestra, Anomalus, Ormorok the Tree-Shaper, Keristrasza.

Long-ish, four bosses in a circular layout.

- **Tank:** **Telestra** splits into 3 elementals (fire/frost/arcane) at 50% HP — tank all three (they don't hit hard), cleave; **all three must die within 5 seconds** or she revives. **Anomalus** spawns rifts that summon adds — ignore rifts, pure boss. **Ormorok** **Trample**s and spawns spike adds — move during Trample. **Keristrasza** ice-blocks the room — she enrages periodically.
- **DPS:** On Telestra, **stack DoTs / Bladestorm to AoE all three** — equal damage = simultaneous death. On Keristrasza, **break ice blocks immediately** by moving (she ice-blocks players who stand still too long).
- **Loot (heroic):** **Stave of Shrouded Mysteries** (Keristrasza HC — caster staff), **Nexus War Champion's Belt** (warrior plate belt), **Helm of the Ancient Tomes** (caster).

## Dragonblight & Grizzly Hills

### Azjol-Nerub (AN)

- **Level range:** 72–74 — Dragonblight. Short, three bosses.
- **Bosses:** Krik'thir the Gatewatcher, Hadronox, Anub'arak.

Fast dungeon. Krik'thir has **3 spider lieutenants** that you can pick off before pulling him.

- **Tank:** **Krik'thir** has watching mobs that pulse damage if not aggro'd — pull them in waves, kill cleanly. **Hadronox** event is a *defensive* fight — adds (Anub'ar Drone, Champion, Crypt Fiend) climb up at you while a giant spider beneath holds them off. Tank stays at the top, AoE adds. **Anub'arak** burrows and **Pound** stuns — Shield Wall during pounds. He spawns scarabs at 75/50/25%.
- **DPS:** Bladestorm the scarab waves on Anub'arak. On Hadronox, prioritize the Crypt Fiends (heal) > Champions > Drones.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Ring of Earthen Might** (Anub'arak HC — tank ring), **Spaulders of Surging Power** (caster). Anub'arak HC is also a **good emblem farm** before raids.

### Ahn'kahet: The Old Kingdom (OK)

- **Level range:** 72–75 — Dragonblight (lower entrance, not the same as AN).
- **Bosses:** Elder Nadox, Prince Taldaram, Jedoga Shadowseeker, Herald Volazj.

Cult-of-the-Damned theme. Volazj is the standout fight.

- **Tank:** **Nadox** has **Swarmer adds** that buff him — kill adds, not boss. **Taldaram** **Vanish + Embrace of the Vampyr** (drains a player) — if you're not the target, **DPS the orbs** he summons or break the channel. **Jedoga** flies up and one-shots an add as a sacrifice — interrupt the wrong one. **Volazj** does **Insanity** — sends each party member into their own dimension where the *other 4 party members appear as enemies* — kill the illusions in your dimension to escape.
- **DPS:** Insanity is the boss check — **must kill all 4 illusions** before they kill you. Bladestorm clears them in seconds. Save it for that.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Spaulders of the Black Arrow** (Volazj HC — leather, skip), **Wraps of Quickening** (cloth, skip). Mostly transmog and emblems.

### Drak'Tharon Keep (DTK)

- **Level range:** 73–76 — Grizzly Hills / Zul'Drak border.
- **Bosses:** Trollgore, Novos the Summoner, King Dred, The Prophet Tharon'ja.

- **Tank:** **Trollgore** stacks a buff per troll he eats — burn fast. **Novos** is invulnerable until you kill **4 channelers on the platform corners** — DPS off-tank or all-AoE. **King Dred** spawns raptor adds — tank picks up, they hit hard if neglected. **Tharon'ja** transforms party into **skeletons** for phase 2 — you only have 3 abilities; use **Decay Strike** as primary.
- **DPS:** Novos's channelers are the priority — Bladestorm them. On Tharon'ja phase 2, you get a basic skeleton ability bar; just spam.
- **Loot (heroic):** **The Prophet's Cudgel** (Tharon'ja HC — Arms 2H mace), **Sceptre of the Devourer** (caster).

### The Violet Hold (VH)

- **Level range:** 73–76 — Dalaran (the city itself).
- **Bosses:** Cyanigosa is the final, plus **2 random mini-bosses** (out of 6 possible) chosen each run.

Wave defense fight — defend the prison through 18 waves of trash plus the 2 random + 1 final boss.

- **Tank:** **Stay at the central pillar.** Adds spawn from 4 portal locations — call out the spawn and Charge → Thunder Clap → Cleave each. **Save Shockwave for the boss waves**. Random bosses each have one mechanic to know — Erekem (interrupt his Earth Shock), Ichoron (kill water bubbles fast), Lavanthor (move out of fire pool), Zuramat (kill void sentries), Moragg (face away — eye beams), Xevozz (interrupt orb sphere chain).
- **DPS:** Bladestorm has a 1.5min CD — perfect for boss waves. Don't waste it on trash. **Cyanigosa** does **Mana Detonation** if there's mana around her — kill her fast or kite.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Tor's Crest** (warrior trinket from Cyanigosa HC — strong), **Cyanigosa's Ring** (caster), **Strapping Mountaineer's Spaulders** (mail).

## Zul'Drak

### Gundrak (Gun)

- **Level range:** 76–79.
- **Bosses:** Slad'ran, Drakkari Colossus, Moorabi, Gal'darah, plus **Eck the Ferocious** (heroic only, optional).

Tile-floor central hub with bosses on each branch.

- **Tank:** **Slad'ran** spawns snake adds that wrap players (root) — break with Berserker Rage / interrupt his cast. **Colossus** has a phase-2 elemental form (after he dies once) — same fight, different DPS check. **Moorabi** transforms into a mammoth (massive damage); **interrupt the transform cast** with War Stomp / Pummel. **Gal'darah** transforms into a rhino, charges, and impales — face him sideways to the group, dodge charge.
- **DPS:** Snake-add AoE on Slad'ran. **Interrupt Moorabi's transform every cast** — if it goes off, expect a fast wipe. Bladestorm is excellent on Gal'darah's between-phase adds.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Plate Belt of the Drakkari** (Gal'darah HC — strong tank belt), **Ouroboros Belt** (caster), **Egg-warming Blanket** (cloth, skip).

### Halls of Stone (HoS)

- **Level range:** 76–79 — Storm Peaks.
- **Bosses:** Krystallus, Maiden of Grief, Sjonnir the Ironshaper, plus **Tribunal of Ages** event.

Dungeon has a **Tribunal escort event** — you defend Brann Bronzebeard while he reads runes. Multiple add waves.

- **Tank:** **Krystallus** has **Stomp** (knockback + party-wide damage) — back-to-wall. **Maiden** drops **Shock of Sorrow** AoE; spread out. **Tribunal** is the gauntlet — hold center, AoE adds for ~5 mins (4 phases). Don't let Brann die. **Sjonnir** spawns iron sludge that combines into bigger adds — kill them while small.
- **DPS:** Tribunal is **Bladestorm heaven** — big add waves, low movement. Save Recklessness for the third wave (hardest).
- **Loot (heroic):** **Brann's Signet Ring** (caster), **Tor's Hammer** (Sjonnir HC — Arms 2H mace), **Belt of Tasseled Lanterns** (mail, skip).

### Halls of Lightning (HoL)

- **Level range:** 77–80.
- **Bosses:** General Bjarngrim, Volkhan, Ionar, Loken.

Hardest of the Storm Peaks duo. **Loken** is the famous tank check.

- **Tank:** **Bjarngrim** rotates between **3 stances** (Defensive, Battle, Berserker) — he hits harder in Berserker; pop Shield Block. **Volkhan** spawns **Molten Golems** that explode if not killed; AoE them down before he shatters them. **Ionar** does **Disperse** (splits into 6 sparks across the room — they slowly converge on players) — DPS the sparks. **Loken** does **Lightning Nova** — pulsing AoE that scales with distance from him; **stay melee** (counter-intuitive, but the closer the better) and the damage is manageable. He also fears — Berserker Rage / War Stomp.
- **DPS:** **On Loken, stay glued to him.** This is the #1 cause of HoL wipes. Don't let his Pulsing Shockwave nova hit you from range. Bladestorm immune to fear, useful when he casts Lightning Nova, you keep doing damage.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Loken's Lightning-Wreathed Mantle** (caster), **Tempered Saronite Helm** (warrior tank helm — strong pre-raid), **Spaulders of the Thunderlord** (Bjarngrim HC — plate DPS).

## Icecrown / Storm Peaks summit

### The Oculus (Oc)

- **Level range:** 76–79.
- **Bosses:** Drakos the Interrogator, Varos Cloudstrider, Mage-Lord Urom, Ley-Guardian Eregos.

**The dragon dungeon.** After Drakos, the entire group **mounts up** on different colored drakes (red/green/amber) for the rest of the instance. Ground gameplay completely changes to drake combat.

- **Tank:** **Drakos** is normal melee — knocks back, big slow. After him you're on a drake.
  - **Red Drake** (tank): Threat-based, has Shield/Damage Reduction. **Pick this**.
  - **Amber Drake** (DPS): Heavy single-target damage.
  - **Green Drake** (heal): Heals allied drakes.
- **Drake combat:** **Varos** has dragon adds; pick up adds with Red Drake's threat ability. **Urom** teleports the group — *dismount* before his nuke or you die mounted. **Eregos** is the final dragon — phase-based, drakes work as a team.
- **DPS:** If you're DPSing, **Amber drake's Shock Lance** during Stop-the-arcane-runes phase. Don't go OOM on the long fights.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Reins of the Blue Drake / Reins of the Azure Drake** — mount drops from Eregos HC (~3-5%; the major reason to farm Oculus). **Annhylde's Ring** (caster).

### Utgarde Pinnacle (UP)

- **Level range:** 78–80. Significantly harder than UK.
- **Bosses:** Svala Sorrowgrave, Gortok Palehoof, Skadi the Ruthless, King Ymiron.

**One of the most-farmed dungeons** because **Skadi drops the Reins of the Blue Proto-Drake**, the meta-achievement reward.

- **Tank:** **Svala** ascends to a platform, drains a random player (sacrifice), then returns. **Gortok Palehoof** has **4 mini-bosses** that spawn one at a time before him; tank rotates through. **Skadi** is the famous **air phase** — flies on a drake, harpoon spears spawn, players use harpoons to knock him off. After landing, ground phase is a normal fight + adds. **Ymiron** has 4 thrones — he activates one at a time, granting different effects; he also fears.
- **DPS:** **Skadi's air phase**: **pick up harpoons quickly** (3 hits to land him, then you have a brief window before he flies again — use cooldowns ground phase). Bladestorm on Palehoof's adds and Ymiron's add waves.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Justicebringer 3000** (caster wand), **Webbed Death** (caster). The big draw is **Reins of the Blue Proto-Drake** from the **Glory of the Hero** meta-achievement (uses UP HC's "King's Bane" achievement, among many).

### The Culling of Stratholme (CoS)

- **Level range:** 78–80 — Caverns of Time.
- **Bosses:** Meathook, Salramm the Fleshcrafter, Chrono-Lord Epoch, Mal'Ganis. Plus the **infinite event** (Infinite Corruptor for the timed-run mount).

You play through Arthas's purge of Stratholme. **Long event-based dungeon** — Arthas leads the group through 10 waves of zombies between bosses.

- **Tank:** Each wave: Arthas calls a round, undead spawn from buildings, you AoE. Arthas helps DPS — let him tank some hits. **Mal'Ganis** is the final — does **Sleep** (random target unconscious for 8 sec), **Vampiric Touch** (drain), and a **Carrion Swarm** (frontal cone — sidestep).
- **DPS:** **Infinite Corruptor** is a hidden timed boss — to spawn him, complete the dungeon in **under 25 minutes**, killing every wave fast. He drops the **Bronze Drake mount** (heroic only). This is a Bladestorm dungeon — every wave is an AoE pull.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Reins of the Bronze Drake** (Infinite Corruptor — only on timed run), **Justice's Hand** (Mal'Ganis HC — Arms 2H sword), **Mal'Ganis's Helmet** (rare drop, plate).

## Patch 3.2 — Trial of the Champion

### Trial of the Champion (ToC 5)

- **Level range:** 80 (Heroic = ilvl 213 loot).
- **Bosses:** Grand Champions (4 random class champions, mounted then dismounted), Argent Confessor Paletress / Eadric the Pure (random), Black Knight.

Faction-flavored — Horde fights different champion roster than Alliance.

- **Tank:** **Grand Champions Phase 1**: mounted joust mechanic — use the Argent Lance, **shield charges** to 3 stacks then ram. **Phase 2**: dismounted, 3 enemy champions all alive — kite the rogue (Bladestorm Whirlwind), tank the warrior, healer (priest/druid) as priority kill. **Paletress** summons a Memory of an old boss (random); kill memory before damage on her. **Eadric** does **Radiance** (eye-flash AoE damage if you face him); **face away** during cast. **Black Knight** has 3 phases (humanoid → skeleton → ghost) — kite ghost phase or burn through it.
- **DPS:** Joust phase: ram only when shield on opponent breaks. Bladestorm is busted in this dungeon — great on champions, great on Black Knight's ghoul phase 2.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Helmet of the Lower Citadel** (warrior tank helm, strong pre-ICC), **Tankard O' Terror** (caster trinket), **Black Knight's Crest** trinket.

## Patch 3.3 — Frozen Halls (ICC 5-mans)

The three patch-3.3 dungeons that **gate-progressed** before ICC raid release. They drop **ilvl 219 (normal) / 232 (heroic)** loot — *raid-tier* gear from 5-mans. Run them on every alt.

### Forge of Souls (FoS)

- **Level range:** 80.
- **Bosses:** Bronjahm, Devourer of Souls.

Short, two bosses. Both heavy on movement.

- **Tank:** **Bronjahm** does **Corrupted Soul Fragments** (orbs that float around, soulstorm phase) and **Magic Bane** (random target, big damage); he also fears — Berserker Rage. **Devourer of Souls** does **Mirrored Soul** (damage you deal goes to the boss too — *stop attacking if pop'd on you*), **Phantom Blast** (interrupt!), and **Wailing Souls** (rotating beam — circle around the boss).
- **DPS:** **Mirrored Soul** = stop DPS for 8 sec or you kill yourself. On Wailing Souls, **strafe around** — Bladestorm runs through it.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Cudgel of Furious Justice** (Bronjahm HC — Arms 1H mace, BiS-tier), **Reaper of Dark Souls** (Devourer HC — 2H scythe/polearm), **Soulthief's Braided Belt** (caster).

### Pit of Saron (PoS)

- **Level range:** 80.
- **Bosses:** Forgemaster Garfrost, Ick & Krick, Scourgelord Tyrannus.

Long with **escort sections** (Saurfang) and big trash pulls. Probably the most run heroic in 3.3.

- **Tank:** **Garfrost** drops **Saronite boulders** the group hides behind to LoS his Permafrost (raid-wide damage); **kite** behind boulders, position yourself. **Ick & Krick** — Krick rides Ick; Ick has **Pursuit** (random target chase, he's faster than you — *run in a straight line*) and **Mighty Kick** (1-shot frontal). **Tyrannus** is mounted on Rimefang (frost wyrm); ground tank Tyrannus, **DPS deals with Rimefang's frost patches**.
- **DPS:** On Ick, **whoever is "Pursued" by name in chat must run away** — Ick chases, hits massively if he catches. On Tyrannus, **interrupt Overlord's Brand** (it reflects damage). Bladestorm on the gauntlet.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Cloak of Many Skins** (caster), **Cuffs of Frost-Bound Souls** (cloth), **Tyrannical Beheader** (Tyrannus HC — 2H axe, **BiS-tier Arms weapon pre-ICC**).

### Halls of Reflection (HoR)

- **Level range:** 80. **The hardest 5-man in the game** for a fresh tank.
- **Bosses:** Falric, Marwyn, **Lich King escape**.

Wave-defense + boss + escape gauntlet. **8 waves of trash**, each ending with adds, then bosses Falric → Marwyn → Lich King chase phase.

- **Tank:** **Falric/Marwyn intro** — stand near Jaina/Sylvanas, hold the line as 8 waves of named adds spawn. The named adds include **Soul Reaver** (knocks back, hits hard), **Soulguard Bloodmage** (heal — kill first), **Phantom Mage** (channels frostbolt), **Phantom Hallucination** (rogue-style, vanish/ambush). **Falric** does **Fear** + adds, **Marwyn** does **Corrupt Flesh** (heavy DoT — dispel via Berserker Rage doesn't work, healer must spam). **Lich King phase**: he's invulnerable, you **run** while he chases; ice walls slow him. **Don't engage**, just sprint with the NPC.
- **DPS:** **Kill priority**: Bloodmages > Phantom Mages > Hallucinations > Soul Reavers. Bladestorm on every wave — **3 charges in 1.5 min** is enough. On the LK chase, **DPS the ice walls** — 4 walls between safe spots; they have HP, party shreds them.
- **Loot (heroic):** **Citadel Enforcer's Claymore** (Marwyn HC — 2H sword, **literal BiS pre-ICC for Arms**), **Bone Fever Gauntlets** (warrior plate hands), **Royal Crest of Lordaeron** (caster).

## Strategy: which dungeons to farm at 80

**Pre-raid BiS sources for warriors (the short list):**

| Slot | Item | Source |
|------|------|--------|
| Helm | Helmet of the Lower Citadel | ToC 5 HC |
| Neck | Skull-shaped Coin | Pit of Saron HC quest reward |
| Shoulders | Tempered Mercurial Pauldrons | World drop / crafted |
| Cloak | Bloodbane's Resolve | Forge of Souls HC |
| Chest | Breastplate of the Solemn Council | Wyrmrest Accord rep |
| Wrist | Bracers of Reverence | Halls of Reflection HC |
| Hands | Bone Fever Gauntlets | Halls of Reflection HC |
| Belt | Belt of the Glacial Wanderer | World drop / crafted |
| Legs | Pillars of Might | Crafted |
| Feet | Sabatons of the Endless Hunt | Trial of the Champion HC |
| Ring | Ring of Earthen Might | Azjol-Nerub HC |
| Trinket | Black Heart | Trial of the Champion HC |
| Trinket | Glyph of Indomitability | Triumph emblems vendor |
| 1H Weapon | Cudgel of Furious Justice | Forge of Souls HC |
| Shield | Bulwark of Smouldering Steel | Triumph emblems vendor |
| Ranged | Bouldercrag's War Horn | Sons of Hodir rep |

**Routine dungeon farm priority:**
1. **Forge of Souls / Pit of Saron / Halls of Reflection HC** — emblems + raid-tier loot.
2. **Trial of the Champion HC** — trinket + helm + boots.
3. **Random Heroic** (RDF) for the **2 Frost Emblems** daily bonus.
4. **Utgarde Pinnacle / Halls of Lightning HC** — stragglers for **Glory of the Hero** meta if going for the **Red Proto-Drake**.

That's the loop. Cap your weekly **Frost Emblems** via the random heroic queue, fill in slots from FoS/PoS/HoR, and you'll be raid-ready for ICC in a couple of weeks of casual play.
