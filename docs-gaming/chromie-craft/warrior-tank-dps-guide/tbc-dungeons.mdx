# TBC Dungeons (58–72)

Outland 5-mans. They're tighter, faster, and lean harder on **interrupts and crowd control** than vanilla — TBC is when blizzard introduced the "you must CC or wipe" pull design. Warrior interrupt budget (Pummel + Shield Bash + War Stomp + Concussion Blow + Shockwave once you have it) is enough to handle most TBC pulls without external CC.

> Heroic versions of every TBC dungeon exist. They require **Honored or Revered** with the relevant faction (Cenarion Expedition, Lower City, Sha'tar, Keepers of Time, Thrallmar/Honor Hold, Sporeggar, etc.) before you can buy a heroic key. RDF will queue you to normals; you must **manually walk into heroic** with the key.

## Hellfire Citadel

### Hellfire Ramparts (Ramps)

- **Level range:** 58–65 — Hellfire Peninsula. The first dungeon you'll do as a 58 fresh into Outland.
- **Bosses:** Watchkeeper Gargolmar, Omor the Unscarred, Vazruden the Herald + Nazan.

Gentle ramp into Outland. Mostly tank-and-spank.

- **Tank:** Gargolmar has two healer adds before him — pull and CC/kill them first. Omor uses a **Shadow Whip** that pulls one player to him; tank repositions to grab. **Nazan flies in** during Vazruden — switch when he lands.
- **DPS:** Don't stand in fire on Nazan's bombing run. Bladestorm during pulls is excellent, lots of trash packs of 4-5.
- **Loot:** **Royal Cloak of the Sunstriders** (caster), **Felfury Legplates** (BoE world drop, plate leg). **The Sunbreaker** (Vazruden — Arms 2H sword).

### The Blood Furnace (BF)

- **Level range:** 59–65.
- **Bosses:** The Maker, Broggok, Keli'dan the Breaker.

Mind-control mechanics make this notable.

- **Tank:** **The Maker** mind-controls a party member periodically — break with stun/Concussion Blow on the MC'd player. **Broggok** has an add-wave intro: 4 cells of orcs open one by one — tank at the door, AoE clean. **Keli'dan** channels a wipe nuke at low HP — interrupt with War Stomp / Pummel or use the channelers around him to stack Bladestorm immunity.
- **DPS:** Broggok's poison clouds — stay max melee or move out. Save big cooldowns for Keli'dan to interrupt the channel via burst damage.
- **Loot:** **The Maker's Essence** (caster trinket), **Cleaver of the Unforgiving** (Keli'dan — 2H axe), **Sun-Touched Chain Leggings** (mail, skip).

### The Shattered Halls (Shatt H)

- **Level range:** 67–72. Hardest of the Hellfire trio.
- **Bosses:** Grand Warlock Nethekurse, Blood Guard Porung (heroic only), Warbringer O'mrogg, Warchief Kargath Bladefist.

This is the **heroic gauntlet** that scared people off in TBC original — the Executioner Event is a timer (kill all adds before they execute prisoners).

- **Tank:** Nethekurse's **Shadow Cleave** hits hard — Shield Block uptime. **O'mrogg has two heads** that argue (random aggro shifts) — keep both DPS aware. **Kargath** spawns **Shattered Hand Reaver** adds and does **Blade Dance** (random target, fast hits) — Shield Wall through it.
- **DPS:** Executioner gauntlet — Bladestorm absolutely shines. Sweeping Strikes during the orc waves before Kargath.
- **Loot:** **Helm of the Stalwart Defender** (Kargath — top-tier warrior tank helm), **The Sleeper** (2H mace, Arms-viable), **Bloodfist** (1H mace from Kargath — Fury weapon). Heroic drops higher item-level versions.

## Coilfang Reservoir

### The Slave Pens (SP)

- **Level range:** 60–66 — Zangarmarsh.
- **Bosses:** Mennu the Betrayer, Rokmar the Crackler, Quagmirran.

Easy first Zangarmarsh dungeon. Cenarion Expedition rep.

- **Tank:** Mennu **summons totems** — Cleave/Thunder Clap to kill them quick (especially the Healing Stream). **Rokmar** has a poison spit — face him away from the group. **Quagmirran** has acid that pools on the ground; reposition.
- **DPS:** Skull totems first on Mennu. Steady AoE on Quagmirran's adds.
- **Loot:** **Cudgel of Consecration** (Mennu — caster mace), **Twinblade of the Phoenix** (rare BoE 1H sword from trash), **Manimal's Cinch** (mail, skip).

### The Underbog (UB)

- **Level range:** 61–67.
- **Bosses:** Hungarfen, Ghaz'an, Swamplord Musel'ek + Claw, The Black Stalker.

Mushroom theme. Several tank-heavy bosses.

- **Tank:** Hungarfen periodically grows in size; spawn waves of mushrooms — kite if necessary. **Musel'ek + Bear (Claw)** is two-tank style; you'll grab the bear, party kills Musel'ek. **Black Stalker** levitates random players up; not much you can do about it. Position close to the center.
- **DPS:** AoE the mushrooms. Save Recklessness for the Bear — it has a *lot* of HP.
- **Loot:** **Ironstrider of Urgency** (rare BoE leg armor, plate), **Mark of the Swarm** (caster trinket).

### The Steamvault (SV)

- **Level range:** 66–72.
- **Bosses:** Hydromancer Thespia, Mekgineer Steamrigger, Warlord Kalithresh.

Lower City + Cenarion Expedition rep, plus the **Karazhan attunement** ends here on the original questline.

- **Tank:** **Thespia** has a water elemental add (Coilfang Elemental) — pick it up, focus boss. She also enrages at low HP. **Steamrigger** spawns repair gnomes; **must die** or he heals. AoE them. **Kalithresh** uses tubes to **massively buff himself** — break the tubes with quick DPS swaps (3 tubes around the room).
- **DPS:** AoE Steamrigger's repair gnomes the moment they spawn. On Kalithresh, **switch to tubes** the moment he runs to one — any DPS time spent on his channel is wasted.
- **Loot:** **Earthwarden** (Hydromancer — 2H mace, *amazing* for a feral druid; tank/Arms can use), **The Steamcudgel** (1H mace, Arms-viable).

## Auchindoun

### Mana-Tombs (MT)

- **Level range:** 62–68 — Terokkar Forest.
- **Bosses:** Pandemonius, Tavarok, Nexus-Prince Shaffar, plus Yor (heroic only).

Sha'tar + Consortium rep. Tighter pulls of casters.

- **Tank:** Pandemonius is **immune to physical periodically** (Void Shield) — stop attacking, move on. **Shaffar** summons **Ethereum Apprentices** that channel a heal — AoE/cleave them down fast.
- **DPS:** Skull the apprentices on Shaffar pulls. Don't burn cooldowns during Pandemonius's Void Shield.
- **Loot:** **Stillwater Boots** (mail, skip), **Talon of Vornskr** (caster). Mostly transmog at this level.

### Auchenai Crypts (AC)

- **Level range:** 63–69.
- **Bosses:** Shirrak the Dead Watcher, Exarch Maladaar.

Lower City rep. Notable for the **D'sparil event** as a rare alternative.

- **Tank:** **Shirrak** casts a debuff (**Focus Fire**) that increases your cast time — for warriors this barely matters. He also summons mini-watchers that latch onto party members; pull them off via taunt or quick burst. **Maladaar** uses **Soul Scream** (silence) and summons a **Doppelganger** of a random player who copies their abilities; tank picks it up.
- **DPS:** Don't let Shirrak's adds chase the healer — peel via Intervene or Cleave them off.
- **Loot:** **Scarab of Displacement** (caster trinket), **Greaves of Shackled Souls** (Maladaar — plate, decent for fresh 70 tank).

### Sethekk Halls (SH)

- **Level range:** 65–71.
- **Bosses:** Darkweaver Syth, Talon King Ikiss. Heroic adds **Anzu** via the **Raven Lord mount** event (druid-only summon item, but mount drops to anyone in the group).

Lower City rep. Also where you eventually come to farm Anzu for the Raven Lord mount post-70.

- **Tank:** Syth summons **4 elementals** (fire/frost/arcane/shadow) at 75% HP — they hit hard, AoE down. **Ikiss** does **Arcane Explosion** that wipes if not interrupted/LoS'd — when he says his line, **run behind a pillar**. Polymorph too — Berserker Rage breaks any incidental fear.
- **DPS:** On Ikiss, when he stops moving and starts casting, **break LoS immediately**. Bladestorm immune to the cast effects but you still need to be in range.
- **Loot:** **Talon King's Coronet** (Ikiss — caster), **Cloak of the Inciter** (Syth — caster). Anzu drops the **Raven Lord mount** in heroic.

### Shadow Labyrinth (Shadow Lab / SL)

- **Level range:** 68–72. Hardest Auchindoun.
- **Bosses:** Ambassador Hellmaw, Blackheart the Inciter, Grandmaster Vorpil, Murmur.

Lower City rep. Murmur is *specifically* a tank check — biggest hitter at the level.

- **Tank:** **Hellmaw** fears periodically — Berserker Rage / racial War Stomp resets. **Blackheart** mind-controls the entire party (yes, including the tank); save Shield Wall for when he releases you, you'll come back to a frenzied healer/DPS. **Vorpil** teleports the whole party to him — keep them grouped, AoE the void travelers. **Murmur** does **Sonic Boom** (massive raid-wide damage), **Murmur's Touch** (debuff that explodes — debuffed player runs out), and **Resonance** (chain damage, spread out).
- **DPS:** On Murmur, **3 mechanics simultaneously**: spread for Resonance, run out for Touch, group up to LoS Sonic Boom against a pillar. Bladestorm covers all three because it's mobile damage immune to most effects.
- **Loot:** **Mallet of the Tides** (Murmur — Arms 1H mace), **Cataclysm Cloak** (Murmur — caster), **Shoulderpads of Assassination** (rogue/Fury).

## Caverns of Time

### Old Hillsbrad Foothills (OHF / Durnholde)

- **Level range:** 66–71.
- **Bosses:** Lieutenant Drake, Captain Skarloc, Epoch Hunter (escort Thrall).

Keepers of Time rep. **Long escort phases** — you protect young Thrall through a series of fights.

- **Tank:** **Phase 1** — Thrall's barn breakout, fight wave-by-wave. **Phase 2** — Thrall rides ahead, fight Skarloc as Thrall arrives. **Phase 3** — wagon section to Tarren Mill, defend Thrall; **Epoch Hunter** spawns at the end.
- **DPS:** Sweeping Strikes / Bladestorm on the wagon section is gold. Don't pull more than one camp at a time — Thrall's HP is the timer.
- **Loot:** **Edge of the Tuskarr** (Skarloc — 2H axe), **Thrall's Resolve** (caster trinket reward via quest).

### The Black Morass (BM / Dark Portal)

- **Level range:** 68–72.
- **Bosses:** Chrono Lord Deja, Temporus, Aeonus, plus 18 portal waves of trash.

Keepers of Time rep. **Defend Medivh as he opens the Dark Portal** through 18 escalating waves of demons + 3 mini-bosses at fixed points.

- **Tank:** **Set up at the fountain** near Medivh. Each portal spawns adds that run at Medivh; you need to grab them fast or his HP burns. **Bosses appear at portals 6, 12, and 18** — Deja, Temporus (does **Hasten** raid-wide haste debuff = healer dies), and **Aeonus** (does **Sand Breath**, frontal fire AoE). **Always face Aeonus away** from the group.
- **DPS:** Bladestorm on portal waves is *the* move. Save it for the bigger packs. Burst on bosses when they spawn.
- **Loot:** **Continuum Blade** (Aeonus — 2H sword), **Hourglass of the Unraveller** (rare trinket from Temporus, caster).

## Tempest Keep

### The Botanica (Bot)

- **Level range:** 67–72 — Netherstorm.
- **Bosses:** Commander Sarannis, High Botanist Freywinn, Thorngrin the Tender, Laj, Warp Splinter.

Sha'tar rep. Long, dense pulls.

- **Tank:** **Freywinn turns into a tree** that periodically heals himself + roots — DPS through, save big CDs for the rooted phase. **Thorngrin** enrages periodically. **Warp Splinter** spawns 6 little adds (Warp Splinterlings) at intervals — tank grabs, AoE.
- **DPS:** Cleave/Whirlwind heaven on Warp Splinter. On Freywinn, focus boss; tree adds die fast in cleave.
- **Loot:** **Mooncleaver** (Sarannis — 2H axe), **Brooch of Heightened Potential** (caster trinket), **Earthsoul Britches** (mail, skip).

### The Mechanar (Mech)

- **Level range:** 68–72.
- **Bosses:** Gyro-Kill, Mechano-Lord Capacitus, Sepethrea, Pathaleon the Calculator.

Sha'tar rep. **Mechano-Lord Capacitus drops the head** for an attunement-era quest.

- **Tank:** Capacitus fires off **Polarity Shift** that flips group polarity — if you don't reposition, you take a wipe. Watch your buff icon. **Sepethrea** has fire elementals (Sun-Forged Warriors) that immune to fire — adds AoE. **Pathaleon** clones a random player and spawns void adds.
- **DPS:** Polarity Shift means **stack with same-polarity** players. Save bursts for after polarity sorts. Bladestorm during Pathaleon's add phase.
- **Loot:** **Manual of the Planes** (Pathaleon — caster trinket), **Cudgel of Consecration** (caster mace), **Belt of the Forgotten Conqueror** (token).

### The Arcatraz (Arc)

- **Level range:** 69–72. Hardest Tempest Keep dungeon by reputation.
- **Bosses:** Zereketh the Unbound, Dalliah the Doomsayer, Wrath-Scryer Soccothrates, Harbinger Skyriss + Warden Mellichar event.

Sha'tar rep, gates the **Karazhan attunement** in TBC original. The **Warden Mellichar event** is 5 cell-summons of mini-bosses, then the final.

- **Tank:** **Dalliah** does **Whirlwind** (move out — she stops casting if you do). **Soccothrates** does **Knockback** (charges you, position back-to-wall). **Mellichar event** = adds galore — Bladestorm/AoE. **Skyriss** clones himself (one real, one fake); cleave both safely until the real one is identified by health drop.
- **DPS:** Move out of Dalliah's Whirlwind. On Skyriss, if you cleave both clones equally you'll find the real one passively. Bladestorm Mellichar's pre-fight adds.
- **Loot:** **Light's Justice** (Skyriss — 1H mace, holy paladin BiS), **Soccothrates' Belt** (mail, skip), **Telric's Tutorial** (caster offhand).

### Magisters' Terrace (MgT)

- **Level range:** 68–72 (designed for 70 Heroic). **Sunwell-era 5-man, 3.0 patch.**
- **Bosses:** Selin Fireheart, Vexallus, Priestess Delrissa + 4 random adds, Kael'thas Sunstrider.

Shattered Sun Offensive rep. **Drops the Phoenix Hatchling pet** from Kael'thas, plus **best pre-raid gear of TBC** for 70 twinks.

- **Tank:** **Selin** drains crystals to refill mana — interrupt his channel or break the crystals first. **Vexallus** spawns lightning adds; pure AoE. **Delrissa** is a **PvP-style 5-target encounter** — she has 4 random class adds (priest/rogue/druid/etc.). Mark a kill order, focus the **healer** (priest/druid) first. **Kael'thas** has 3 phases — **Pyroblast** (interrupt!), **Gravity Lapse** (you fly up randomly, dodge orbs while in air), **Phoenix Phase** (kill phoenix add).
- **DPS:** **Pyroblast on Kael MUST be interrupted** — it kills the tank. Bladestorm immune during Gravity Lapse on the ground. Phoenix add must die before it explodes (timer).
- **Loot:** **Hammer of Judgement** (Kael'thas — 1H, BiS-tier holy/Arms tank weapon for the bracket), **Phoenix Hatchling** (vanity pet), **Cosmic Lifeband** (caster ring), **Vengeance of the Illidari** (trinket).
