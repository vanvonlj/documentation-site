# Blackwing Lair

**Blackwing Lair** can be found at the very height of [Blackrock Spire](https://www.wowhead.com/zone=1583/blackrock-spire) and is a 40-man raid instance. It is there in the dark recesses of the mountain's peak that Nefarian has begun to unfold the final stages of his plan to destroy [Ragnaros](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=11502/ragnaros) once and for all and lead his army to undisputed supremacy over all the races of Azeroth. To this end, he has recently begun efforts to bolster his forces, much as his father Deathwing had attempted to do in ages past.

All bosses in **Blackwing Lair** drop pieces of the [Tier 2 Raid Set](https://www.wowhead.com/itemsets&filter=ta=4), excluding the _leg_, which drop from [Ragnaros](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=11502/ragnaros). Tier 2 helms used to drop from Onyxia, but now drop from [Nefarian](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=11583/nefarian) after Onyxia's revamp in 3.2.2.

 [![Elementium Ingot](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_stone_sharpeningstone_01.gif) Elementium Ingot](https://www.wowhead.com/item=18562/elementium-ingot) is found as a rare drop from the [Blackwing Technician](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=13996/blackwing-technician) that inhabit the lair. This ore is used to smelt [![Enchanted Elementium Bar](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_ingot_thorium.gif) Enchanted Elementium Bar](https://www.wowhead.com/item=17771/enchanted-elementium-bar), needed for [![Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of the Windseeker](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_sword_39.gif) Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of the Windseeker](https://www.wowhead.com/item=19019/thunderfury-blessed-blade-of-the-windseeker). Players can also learn [![Goblin's Guide to Elementium](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_misc_book_11.gif) Goblin's Guide to Elementium](https://www.wowhead.com/item=44956/goblins-guide-to-elementium).

Many players will want to run **Blackwing Lair** for the distinctive weapons, including [![Claw of the Black Drake](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_weapon_shortblade_27.gif) Claw of the Black Drake](https://www.wowhead.com/item=19365/claw-of-the-black-drake), [![Dragonbreath Hand Cannon](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_weapon_rifle_02.gif) Dragonbreath Hand Cannon](https://www.wowhead.com/item=19368/dragonbreath-hand-cannon), and [![Chromatically Tempered Sword](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_sword_51.gif) Chromatically Tempered Sword](https://www.wowhead.com/item=19352/chromatically-tempered-sword).
