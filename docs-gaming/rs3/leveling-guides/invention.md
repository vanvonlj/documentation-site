# Invention Leveling Guide

Comprehensive guide for training Invention from level 1 to 120 in RuneScape 3. Invention is an Elite skill that enhances equipment with powerful perks.

## Why Train Invention?

- **Perks** - Add powerful effects to weapons and armor
- **Equipment XP** - Gain Invention XP while doing other activities
- **Money Saving** - Perks like Scavenging and Prosper save/make money
- **DPS Increase** - Essential for high-level PvM
- **Required for:** Trimmed Completionist Cape

## Requirements to Unlock

- **80 Divination**
- **80 Crafting**
- **80 Smithing**
- Complete "Invention" tutorial

## How Invention Works

### Basic Process
1. **Augment** equipment with an augmentor
2. **Use** the augmented equipment to gain item XP
3. **Disassemble** at level 10 for maximum XP
4. **OR Siphon** at level 12 to keep the item (less XP)

### Equipment Levels
- Items gain XP as you use them
- Level 10: Disassemble for best XP
- Level 12: Siphon for partial XP (keeps item)
- Level 20: Absolute maximum

## Training Methods by Level

### Levels 1-27: Discovering Blueprints
**Method:** Discover all basic blueprints
- **XP:** 500k-1M total
- **Cost:** 1-2M GP
- **Time:** 1-2 hours
- Discover items in your Invention interface

**Key Discoveries:**
- Augmentors
- Gizmos
- Tool gizmo shells
- Weapon/armor gizmo shells

### Levels 27-60: Augmented Weapons/Armor
**Method:** Augment and disassemble equipment
- **XP Rate:** 200k-400k XP/hr
- **Cost:** 10-20M GP
- **Best items:**
  - Black salamanders (cheap)
  - Royal d'hide bodies
  - Crystal halberd (if you have it)

**Process:**
1. Augment items (level 1)
2. Train combat until item level 10
3. Disassemble for Invention XP
4. Repeat

### Levels 60-99: Efficient Training
**Method:** Augment and siphon high-level gear OR disassemble cheaper items
- **XP Rate:** 400k-800k XP/hr (while training combat)
- **Cost:** Variable (5-50M depending on method)

**Option A: Siphon High-Level Gear**
- Augment your main weapons (T80-90)
- Train combat normally
- Siphon at level 12
- Keeps your gear, slower XP

**Option B: Disassemble Black Salamanders**
- Cheap and fast
- Augment → level to 10 → disassemble
- Good for pure Invention training

**Option C: ED3 Training**
- Augment armor and weapons
- Do ED3 trash runs
- Fast combat + item XP
- Best overall method

### Levels 99-120: Master Invention
**Method:** Continue augmenting and siphoning/disassembling
- **XP Rate:** 500k-1M XP/hr (passive while doing other activities)
- **Cost:** Variable

**Best Methods:**
1. **Augment all gear** and siphon at 12
2. **ED3 trash runs** - Fast combat + item XP
3. **Slayer** - Passive training while making money
4. **Disassemble cheap weapons** - Direct training

|------|-----------|------|-----|-------|

### For Siphoning (Keeping Gear)
- Your main weapons and armor
- Siphon at level 12 for ~50% of disassemble XP
- Keeps the item intact

## Important Unlocks

### Essential Discoveries (By Level)
- **Level 1:** Augmentors
- **Level 16:** Equipment siphon
- **Level 22:** Weapon gizmos
- **Level 45:** Armor gizmos
- **Level 49:** Tool gizmos
- **Level 60:** Dwarven tools
- **Level 95:** Ancient gizmos (better perks)

### Ancient Invention (95+)
**Unlock:** Complete "The Dig Site" and reach 95 Invention
- Better gizmos with 9 slots instead of 5
- More powerful perk combinations
- Required for end-game perks

## Best Perks by Slot

### Weapon Perks (DPS Focus)
**Best Combinations:**
1. **Precise 6 + Equilibrium 4** (or P6E2)
2. **Aftershock 4 + Equilibrium 2** (or AS4)

**Budget/Mid-Tier:**
- Precise 5
- Equilibrium 3 or 4
- Aftershock 3

### Armor Perks (Defense/Utility)
**Best Combinations:**
1. **Biting 4 + Mobile** (or Biting 4 alone)
2. **Crackling 4 + Relentless 5** (or Crack 4 + Mobile)
3. **Enhanced Devoted 4**
4. **Impatient 4 + Devoted 4**

**Budget/Mid-Tier:**
- Biting 3
- Crackling 3
- Enhanced Devoted 3
- Impatient 3

### Tool Perks (Skilling)
**Pickaxe/Hatchet:**
- Honed 6 (bonus XP)
- Furnace 4 (auto-smelt ores)
- Prosper (clue scrolls)

**Fishing Rod:**
- Honed 6
- Furnace 4

**Other Tools:**
- Honed 6 for all tools
- Rapid 3 for faster skilling

## Materials for Perks

### Common Materials
- **Armadyl components** - Precise perk
- **Zamorak components** - Equilibrium perk
- **Ilujankan components** - Aftershock perk (expensive)
- **Noxious components** - Biting perk (expensive)
- **Explosive components** - Crackling perk

### How to Get Components
1. **Disassemble items** - Main method
2. **Use scavenging perk** - Passive components while training
3. **Buy from GE** - Disassemble tradeable items

### Cost-Effective Component Sources
- **Armadyl components:** Disassemble Armadyl godsword (expensive) or rune items
- **Zamorak components:** Disassemble Zamorak godsword or subjugation
- **Explosive components:** Disassemble hand cannons

## Invention Training Strategy

### Budget Method (Slow but Cheap)
1. Augment black salamanders or royal d'hide
2. Train in Abyss or ED3
3. Disassemble at level 10
4. Use budget perks

**Cost:** 15-25M GP (1-99)
**Time:** 40-60 hours

### Standard Method (Balanced)
1. Augment your main gear
2. Train combat through Slayer or ED3
3. Siphon at level 12
4. Get mid-tier perks as you level

**Cost:** 25-40M GP (1-99)
**Time:** 30-50 hours (passive while training combat)

### Fast Method (Expensive)
1. Augment high-tier weapons
2. Do ED3 trash runs with augmented gear
3. Disassemble or siphon frequently
4. Get best perks ASAP

**Cost:** 50-100M GP (1-99)
**Time:** 20-30 hours

## Tips for Efficient Training

1. **Augment everything** - Train Invention passively
2. **Siphon vs Disassemble** - Siphon expensive gear, disassemble cheap items
3. **Use ED3** - Fast combat XP + item XP
4. **Scavenging perk** - Get free components while training
5. **Do Slayer** - Money + combat + Invention XP
6. **Discover blueprints** - Free XP, do this early
7. **Ancient Invention** - Unlock at 95 for better perks
8. **Use presets** - Speed up augmenting process

## Common Mistakes

1. **Disassembling expensive gear** - Siphon it instead
2. **Not using augmented gear** - Always train with augmented items
3. **Ignoring perks** - Perks make a huge difference
4. **Waiting to train Invention** - Start early, it's passive
5. **Disassembling at wrong level** - Level 10 for disassemble, 12 for siphon

## Ironman Tips

1. **Make crystal halberds** - Free augmentable items
2. **Use Sunspear** - Free from River of Blood quest
3. **ED3 for components** - Disassemble drops
4. **Scavenging perk** - Essential for components
5. **Be patient** - Much slower without GE

## 99-120 Training

Continue augmenting and siphoning/disassembling.

**XP Required:** 104M total XP for 120
**Time:** 150-250 hours (passive) or 50-100 hours (active)
