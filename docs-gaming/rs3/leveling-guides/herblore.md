# Herblore Leveling Guide

Comprehensive guide for training Herblore from level 1 to 120 in RuneScape 3. Herblore is essential for making potions, especially Overloads at level 96.

## Why Train Herblore?

- **Level 96** - Overloads (essential for PvM)
- **Level 99** - Supreme Overloads
- **Level 106** - Holy Overloads
- **Level 120** - Elder Overloads
- Profitable at high levels
- Required for Plague's End (75 Herblore)

## Training Methods by Level

### Levels 1-26: Attack Potions
**Ingredients:** Guam leaf + Eye of newt
- **Cost:** ~100k GP
- **XP Rate:** 40k-60k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make attack potions
- Very cheap to train

### Levels 26-38: Prayer Potions
**Ingredients:** Ranarr weed + Snape grass
- **Cost:** ~500k GP
- **XP Rate:** 80k-120k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make prayer potions
- Can be profitable if you're careful

### Levels 38-52: Super Attack Potions
**Ingredients:** Irit leaf + Eye of newt
- **Cost:** ~1M GP
- **XP Rate:** 120k-150k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make super attack potions
- Standard mid-level method

### Levels 52-66: Super Energy Potions
**Ingredients:** Avantoe + Mort myre fungus
- **Cost:** ~2M GP
- **XP Rate:** 150k-200k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make super energy potions

**Alternative:** Super Strength (Kwuarm + Limpwurt root)
- Similar cost and XP

### Levels 66-84: Super Defence Potions
**Ingredients:** Cadantine + White berries
- **Cost:** ~5M GP
- **XP Rate:** 200k-250k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make super defence potions

**Alternative:** Weapon Poison+++
- Can be more profitable but similar XP

### Levels 84-96: Adrenaline Potions
**Ingredients:** Super energy (4) + Papaya
- **Cost:** ~15M GP
- **XP Rate:** 350k-450k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make adrenaline potions (combination potion)
- One of the best methods to 96

**Alternative:** Super Prayer Renewal
**Ingredients:** Prayer renewal (4) + Wyvern bonemeal
- Similar XP, sometimes cheaper

### Levels 96-99: Overloads
**Ingredients:** Super attack/strength/defence/ranging/magic + Torstol
- **Cost:** ~20M GP to 99
- **XP Rate:** 450k-550k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make overloads
- **WARNING:** You'll use these, so they're not really a cost

### Levels 99-120: Supreme Overloads
**Ingredients:** Overload (4) + Super prayer renewal (4) + Clean fellstalk
- **Cost:** 50M+ GP
- **XP Rate:** 500k-650k XP/hr
- **Method:** Make supreme overloads
- These are the best combat potions in the game

## Money-Saving Methods

### Farming Your Own Herbs

- **Plant:** Herb seeds (Ranarr, Snapdragon, Torstol)
- **XP:** Passive Farming XP
- **Savings:** 30-50% cost reduction
- **Time:** 2-3 runs per day

Use **Greenfingers aura** and **Juju farming potion** for better yields.

### Profitable Herblore

Some potions can be profitable if you're patient:

1. **Prayer Potions** (26+) - Sometimes profitable
2. **Super Prayer Renewal** (94+) - Can break even
3. **Weapon Poison+++** (82+) - Often profitable
4. **Combination Potions** - Check GE prices

Always check current GE prices before buying ingredients.

## Essential Items & Boosts

### Equipment
- **Portable Well** - 10% extra potions, +10% XP
- **Modified botanist's mask** - 5% chance to make extra potion
- **Factory outfit** - 10% chance to make 4-dose potion
- **Scroll of cleansing** (65 Dungeoneering) - 10% ingredient save

### Auras
- **Greenfingers** - Better herb yields when farming
- **Wisdom** - 2.5% bonus XP (general training aura)

### Other Boosts
- **Botanist's amulet** - 5% chance to make 4-dose potion
- **God banner** - 30 minutes of 2% XP boost per day
- **Herblore urns** - 20% bonus XP

## Making Overloads

Overloads are the #1 reason to train Herblore.

### Requirements
- **96 Herblore** (boostable from 94)
- Super attack (4)
- Super strength (4)
- Super defence (4)
- Super ranging (4)
- Super magic (4)
- Clean torstol

### Why Overloads?
- +15% to combat stats (Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, Ranged)
- Lasts 6 minutes
- Essential for mid-to-high level PvM
- Required for efficient bossing

### Upgrading Overloads

**Supreme Overload (99 Herblore)**
- Overload (4) + Super prayer renewal (4) + Clean fellstalk
- Combines Overload + Prayer renewal
- Best-in-slot for most content

**Holy Overload (106 Herblore)**
- Supreme overload (6) + 4x Prayer renewal (4) + 1x Crystal flask
- 6-dose flask version

**Elder Overload (120 Herblore)**
- Even better stats and duration

## Combination Potions

Combination potions give great XP and are useful:

### Popular Combinations
- **Adrenaline Potion** (84) - Super energy + Papaya
- **Aggression Potion** (82) - Searing ashes + Bloodweed
- **Weapon Poison+++** (82) - Weapon poison++ + Primal extract
- **Super Prayer Renewal** (94) - Prayer renewal + Fellstalk

## Herblore Training Strategy

### Budget Method (Slow)
1. Farm your own herbs
2. Make profitable potions
3. Use all XP boosts
4. Train during Double XP events

**Cost:** 5-10M GP (1-96)
**Time:** 40-60 hours + farming time

### Standard Method
1. Buy herbs from GE
2. Make meta potions (super defence, adrenaline, overloads)
3. Use portable wells and outfit
4. Train consistently

**Cost:** 25-35M GP (1-96)
**Time:** 20-30 hours

### Fast Method
1. Buy everything from GE
2. Use all boosts (well, outfit, amulet, scroll)
3. Make fastest XP/hr potions
4. Don't worry about profit

**Cost:** 40-50M GP (1-99)
**Time:** 15-25 hours

## Herblore Habitat

**Herblore Habitat** is a D&D that gives bonus Herblore XP.

- Unlock by completing "As a First Resort" quest
- Plant jadinkos and catch them for XP
- Can give Farming and Hunter XP too
- Good for low-level training

## Daily Herblore Activities

1. **Herby Werby** (D&D) - Weekly minigame for huge XP
2. **God Statues** (D&D) - Monthly for bonus XP
3. **Jack of Trades Aura** - Free daily XP in Herblore
4. **Daily Challenge** - Random Herblore task for XP

## Tips for Efficient Training

1. **Use Portable Wells** - Find at high-traffic locations (Combat Academy, GE)
2. **Get Factory Outfit** - From Flash Powder Factory minigame
3. **Use Scroll of Cleansing** - Save 10% of ingredients
4. **Make 4-dose Potions** - More efficient than 3-dose
5. **Buy ingredients on GE** - Faster than farming (unless ironman)
6. **Use Presets** - Banking preset for super fast training
7. **Train During DXP** - Double XP weekends save millions
8. **Get 96 for Overloads** - The main goal of Herblore training

## Quest Requirements

### Important Quests
- **Druidic Ritual** - Unlocks Herblore skill
- **Plague's End** (75 Herblore) - Unlocks Prifddinas
- **As a First Resort** - Unlocks Herblore Habitat

## Ironman Tips

1. **Farm everything** - You'll need your own herbs
2. **Do Herblore Habitat** - Good early XP
3. **Save Torstols** - You'll need hundreds for overloads
4. **Do Herby Werby weekly** - Free Herblore XP
5. **Miscellania** - Set kingdom to farm herbs
