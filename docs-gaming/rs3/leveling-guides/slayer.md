# Slayer Leveling Guide

Comprehensive guide for training Slayer from level 1 to 120 in RuneScape 3. Slayer is one of the most profitable and rewarding skills.

## Why Train Slayer?

- **Money Making** - Best consistent GP/hr in the game
- **Combat Training** - Train all combat skills while making money
- **Rare Drops** - Access to unique drops worth millions
- **120 Cape** - Slayer is worth training to 120
- **Reaper Tasks** - Required for end-game PvM

## Training Methods by Level

### Levels 1-50: Getting Started
**Master:** Turael → Mazchna → Vannaka
- **XP Rate:** 10k-50k XP/hr
- **GP/hr:** Minimal
- **Method:** Complete tasks normally, focus on learning
- Quick levels, don't stress efficiency yet

**Key Unlocks:**
- Level 1: Crawling Hands (decent early XP)
- Level 10: Cave bugs
- Level 20: Rockslug
- Level 40: Pyrefiends

### Levels 50-75: Mid-Level Training
**Master:** Chaeldar (50+) → Sumona (75+)
- **XP Rate:** 60k-150k XP/hr
- **GP/hr:** 500k-2M
- **Method:** Start blocking bad tasks, prefer combat tasks

**Good Tasks:**
- Greater Demons (easy, good XP)
- Hellhounds (AFK, decent drops)
- Fire Giants (good drops)
- Dagannoths (varied, profitable)

**Block/Skip:**
- Turoths (low XP)
- Grotworms (low profit)
- Brine rats (annoying)

### Levels 75-85: Pre-Elite Training
**Master:** Sumona → Duradel (85+)
- **XP Rate:** 150k-300k XP/hr
- **GP/hr:** 2M-4M
- **Method:** Block low XP tasks, focus on profitable tasks

**Great Tasks:**
- Abyssal Demons (85+ recommended) - 3-5M GP/hr
- Gargoyles - Good afk GP
- Dark Beasts - Excellent GP
- Greater Demons (Kal'gerion) - Great XP if you can handle them

### Levels 85-99: Elite Slayer
**Master:** Duradel → Kuradal (90+) → Morvran (99+)
- **XP Rate:** 300k-600k XP/hr
- **GP/hr:** 5M-10M+
- **Method:** Optimize with Slayer VIP tickets, block bad tasks

**Best Tasks:**
- **Abyssal Demons** - 600k+ XP/hr, 4M+ GP/hr
- **Corrupted Creatures** (80+) - Very profitable
- **Ripper Demons** (96) - Challenging but amazing drops
- **Sophanem Slayer Dungeon** (88+) - Soul Devourers, good XP/GP
- **Lost Grove Creatures** (90+) - Moss Golems, Vinecrawlers

### Levels 99-120: Master Slayer
**Master:** Morvran → Laniakea (120+)
- **XP Rate:** 400k-800k XP/hr
- **GP/hr:** 8M-15M+
- **Method:** High-level Slayer creatures, Reaper tasks

**Best Tasks:**
- Ripper Demons (96) - Best profit
- Acheron Mammoths (96) - Good XP/GP
- Corrupted Creatures - Consistent money
- Elite Dungeons - Great overall XP
- Lost Grove - Good variety

## Essential Slayer Unlocks

### Must-Have Unlocks (Slayer Points)
1. **Slayer VIP Tickets** - Choice of 2 tasks (hugely important)
2. **Toolbelt** - Store items on toolbelt
3. **Broader Fletching** - Make broad arrows/bolts for profit
4. **Smoking Kills** - 4x Slayer points (unlock ASAP)
5. **Quicker Kills** - 10% Slayer XP boost
6. **Superior Slayer** - Chance for superior variants (better drops)

### Good Unlocks
- **Mask/Helm of Slayer** - Force specific tasks
- **Contract Connoisseur** - Get Raptor tasks
- **Slayer Partner** - XP boost when training with friend
- **Ferocious Ring** - Teleports to Kuradal

### Task Unlocks
- **Like a Boss** - Boss variants of tasks
- **Scions Slayer** - Ripper, Acheron, etc. (very profitable)
- **Roar of Osseous** - Anachronia tasks

## Equipment for Slayer

### General Setup
- **Weapon:** Highest tier you can afford (melee/ranged/magic)
- **Armor:** Slayer armor or GWD armor
- **Ring:** Ring of slaying or better
- **Amulet:** Blood fury / Amulet of souls

### Best-in-Slot Slayer
- **Weapon:** Scythe (melee), Noxious bow (ranged), Noxious staff (magic)
- **Armor:** Masterwork (melee), Sirenic (ranged), Tectonic (magic)
- **Familiar:** Steel Titan or Ripper Demon

### Essential Items
- **Charming imp** - Auto-collect charms
- **Herbicide** - Destroy herbs for farming XP
- **Bonecrusher** - Auto-crush bones for prayer XP
- **Seedicide** - Destroy seeds for farming XP
- **Spring cleaner** - Auto-disassemble drops

## Reaper Tasks

**Unlocked:** After completing "Fate of the Gods" quest

Reaper tasks assign you to kill bosses:
- **XP:** Huge Slayer XP rewards
- **GP:** Boss drops worth millions
- **Points:** Reaper points for powerful unlocks

**Best for:** High-level players (90+ combat)

### Reaper Rewards
- **Hydrix** - Expensive gems
- **Instance cost reduction** - Cheaper boss instances
- **Aura refreshes** - Reset auras
- **Death touch darts** - Instakill any NPC

## Tips for Fast Slayer XP

1. **Use Slayer VIP Tickets** - Get better task choices
2. **Block Low XP Tasks** - Free up task pool
3. **Use AOE Abilities** - Multi-target attacks
4. **Cannon/Oldak** - Extra damage and XP
5. **Aggression Potions** - Auto-aggro monsters
6. **Use Best Master** - Morvran or Laniakea
7. **Complete Smoking Kills** - 4x Slayer points
8. **Wear Slayer Helm** - Bonus damage on task

## Money-Making Strategy

### Maximum GP/hr Method
1. Only do profitable tasks (Rippers, Acheron, Abyssal Demons)
2. Skip everything else
3. Use best gear and abilities
4. Use area loot and cleanup items (spring cleaner, etc.)

**Result:** 8M-15M GP/hr + Slayer XP

### Balanced XP/GP Method
1. Block/skip only the worst tasks
2. Do most tasks assigned
3. Use efficient gear
4. Focus on fun

**Result:** 3M-8M GP/hr + good XP

## Daily Slayer Activities

1. **Reaper Task** - Daily boss assignment (huge XP)
2. **Daily Challenge** - Free Slayer XP
3. **Scarabs** - Collect scarabs on Slayer task in Menaphos
4. **Rush of Blood** - Weekly combat minigame

## 1-99 Training Plan

**Levels 1-50:** Use highest master available, learn mechanics
**Levels 50-75:** Chaeldar → Sumona, start blocking bad tasks
**Levels 75-85:** Sumona → Duradel, focus on profitable tasks
**Levels 85-99:** Duradel → Kuradal → Morvran, optimize for XP/GP

**Estimated Time:** 150-250 hours
**Estimated Profit:** 500M-1B+ GP

## 99-120 Training

Continue with Morvran or use Laniakea (unlocked at 120).

**XP Required:** 104M total XP for 120
**Time:** 200-350 hours
**Profit:** 1B-3B+ GP
