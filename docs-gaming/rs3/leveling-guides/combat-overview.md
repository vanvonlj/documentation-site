# Combat Skills Overview

RuneScape 3 has multiple combat styles, each with dedicated skills. This guide covers all combat skills and the best training methods.

## Combat Triangle

- **Melee** - Attack, Strength, Defence
- **Ranged** - Ranged
- **Magic** - Magic
- **Support** - Constitution, Prayer, Summoning

## Quick Training Recommendations by Level

### Levels 1-20
**Location:** Lumbridge Catacombs, Cows, Chickens
- Very fast at low levels
- Safe areas
- Good for learning basics

### Levels 20-40
**Location:** Hill Giants, Flesh Crawlers, Security Stronghold
- Decent drops
- Good XP rates
- Start using food

### Levels 40-60
**Location:** Experiments, Yaks, Rock Crabs
- AFK-friendly
- Consistent XP
- Low danger

### Levels 60-80
**Location:** Slayer tasks, Abyss, Bandits
- Start doing Slayer for best overall training
- Abyss for fast XP
- Profitable training options available

### Levels 80-99
**Location:** Slayer, ED3 trash runs, Abyssal Demons
- **Elite Dungeon 3** - Best XP/hour
- **Slayer** - Profitable and consistent
- **Shattered Worlds** - Fast combat XP

### Levels 99-120
**Location:** Elite Dungeons, high-level Slayer, bossing
- ED3 continues to be excellent
- High-level Slayer tasks
- Boss practice for money and XP

## Best Training Methods by Style

### Melee Training
1. **ED3 Trash Runs** (80+) - 800k-1.5M XP/hr
2. **Slayer** (All levels) - 400k-700k XP/hr + profit
3. **Abyssal Demons** (85+) - 600k-800k XP/hr + profit
4. **Shattered Worlds** (Any level) - Scalable XP

### Ranged Training
1. **ED3 Trash Runs** (80+) - 800k-1.5M XP/hr
2. **Chinning Abyssal Demons** (85+) - 1M+ XP/hr (expensive)
3. **Slayer** (All levels) - 400k-700k XP/hr + profit
4. **Shattered Worlds** (Any level) - Scalable XP

### Magic Training
1. **ED3 Trash Runs** (80+) - 800k-1.5M XP/hr
2. **Slayer** (All levels) - 400k-700k XP/hr + profit
3. **Abyss** (60+) - 600k-800k XP/hr
4. **Player-owned House** (Any level) - Expensive but very fast

## Constitution (HP)

Constitution levels automatically as you train combat. Every combat XP also gives Constitution XP at a 3:1 ratio.

- No need to train separately
- Levels naturally through all combat
- Reaches 99/120 alongside combat skills

## Combat Equipment Progression

### Early Game (1-50)
- Full Iron/Steel/Mithril/Adamant armor
- Weapons from drops or Grand Exchange
- Focus on training over gear

### Mid Game (50-70)
- Rune armor
- Dragon weapons
- Barrows equipment (70+)
- God Wars Dungeon 1 items

### High Level (70-80)
- Barrows equipment
- GWD1 armor (Bandos, Armadyl, Subjugation)
- Chaotic weapons from Dungeoneering
- Slayer drops

### End Game (80+)
- GWD2 armor (Anima Core)
- T90 weapons (Noxious, Ascensions, Seismics)
- T92 weapons (End-game bossing)
- Elite Dungeon armor and weapons

## Combat Abilities

### Revolution++
- Full automatic ability bar
- Good for learning and AFK training
- Can achieve 80%+ of manual DPS

### Revolution (Manual Thresholds)
- Auto basic abilities
- Manually activate thresholds and ultimates
- 90%+ of full manual DPS

### Full Manual
- Complete control over all abilities
- Highest possible DPS
- Required for high-level PvM

## Important Unlocks

### Essential Abilities
- **Corruption Shot/Blast** (50M+ GP) - Worth it at end-game
- **Greater abilities** - Enhanced versions of basic abilities
- **Codexes** - Various combat improvements

### Useful Items
- **Overloads** (96 Herblore) - Best combat potions
- **Curses** (95 Prayer) - Superior prayer book
- **Pack Yak** (96 Summoning) - 30 inventory slots

## Tips for Fast Combat Training

1. **Do Slayer** - Best overall method for profit + XP
2. **Use ED3** - Fastest pure XP at higher levels
3. **Get good gear** - Weapon > Armor for DPS
4. **Use Revolution++** - Easiest while learning
5. **Join a clan** - Learn from experienced players
6. **Do Daily challenges** - Free bonus XP
7. **Use pulse cores** - 10% XP boost in populated areas
