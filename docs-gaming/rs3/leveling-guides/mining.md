# Mining Leveling Guide

Comprehensive guide for training Mining from level 1 to 120 in RuneScape 3.

## Training Methods by Level

### Levels 1-10: Copper/Tin Ore
**Location:** Lumbridge Swamp, Varrock East
- **XP Rate:** 5k-10k XP/hr
- **Method:** Mine copper or tin ore
- **Cost:** Free
- Very quick to get through

### Levels 10-20: Iron Ore
**Location:** Varrock Southwest Mine, Al Kharid Mine
- **XP Rate:** 15k-20k XP/hr
- **Method:** Mine iron ore
- **Cost:** Free
- Respawns quickly

### Levels 20-40: Iron/Coal
**Location:** Mining Guild (60+ recommended), Al Kharid
- **XP Rate:** 30k-50k XP/hr
- **Method:** Mine iron or coal
- **Cost:** Free
- Coal is profitable

### Levels 40-60: Concentrated Coal/Gold
**Location:** Living Rock Caverns, Crafting Guild basement
- **XP Rate:** 60k-100k XP/hr
- **Method:** Mine concentrated coal or gold ore
- **Cost:** Free
- Watch for aggressive monsters in LRC

### Levels 60-80: Seren Stones (Prifddinas)
**Location:** Prifddinas (requires Plague's End quest)
- **XP Rate:** 150k-250k XP/hr
- **Method:** AFK mining at Seren stones
- **Cost:** Free
- **Best for:** AFK training
- Very low effort, no banking needed

**Alternative:** Concentrated Gold (Living Rock Caverns)
- **XP Rate:** 100k-150k XP/hr
- If you don't have access to Prifddinas

### Levels 80-99: Seren Stones / Alaea Crablets
**Location:** Prifddinas or Uncharted Isles
- **XP Rate:** 200k-300k XP/hr (Seren), 350k-450k XP/hr (Crablets)
- **Method:**
  - Seren Stones: Very AFK
  - Alaea Crablets: More active, requires Arc unlocks
- **Cost:** Free (Seren), requires supplies (Crablets)

### Levels 99-120: Alaea Crablets / Seren Stones
**Location:** Uncharted Isles (Arc) or Prifddinas
- **XP Rate:** 400k-500k XP/hr (Crablets), 250k-300k XP/hr (Seren)
- **Method:** Active or AFK depending on preference
- **Cost:** Varies

## Best Training Locations

### Seren Stones (60+, Prifddinas)
- **Best for:** AFK training
- **Requirements:** Plague's End quest
- **XP/hr:** 150k-300k
- **Pros:**
  - Extremely AFK
  - No banking
  - Corruption chance for bonus XP
  - Voice of Seren bonus (20% extra XP periodically)
- **Cons:** Requires high-level quest

### Alaea Crablets (90+, Arc)
**Location:** Uncharted Isles
- **Best for:** Active, fast XP
- **Requirements:** Arc unlocks
- **XP/hr:** 400k-500k+
- **Pros:**
  - Very fast XP
  - Profitable with right setup
- **Cons:**
  - Not AFK
  - Requires Arc progression

### Living Rock Caverns (40+)
- **Best for:** Mid-level training
- **XP/hr:** 100k-150k
- **Pros:**
  - Concentrated coal and gold
  - Good for ironmen
- **Cons:** Aggressive monsters

### Mining Guild (60+ for full access)
- **Best for:** All levels
- **XP/hr:** Varies by ore
- **Pros:**
  - Invisible boost to Mining level (+7)
  - No competition
  - Safe area
- **Cons:** None really

## Equipment Progression

### Pickaxes
| Level | Pickaxe | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Bronze | Starting pickaxe |
| 10 | Iron | Cheap upgrade |
| 20 | Steel | Minor improvement |
| 30 | Mithril | Still affordable |
| 40 | Adamant | Good mid-game option |
| 50 | Rune | Standard until Dragon |
| 60 | Dragon | Good long-term option |
| 70 | Crystal | Requires Plague's End |
| 80 | Imcando | Best in slot (expensive) |

### Augmented Pickaxe (80+ Invention)
- Add perks for bonus XP and efficiency
- **Honed** perk for bonus XP
- **Furnace** perk for auto-smelting
- **Prosper** for clue scrolls

## Mining Boosts

### Consumables
- **Perfect Juju Mining Potion** - 5% bonus XP + finding gems
- **Stone Spirits** - Double ore when mining
- **Rock-crushing scrimshaw** - 15% increased XP for 3-4 hours

### Equipment
- **Quarrymaster Aura** - Increased mining speed and XP
- **Lava Titan** - +10 invisible Mining level boost
- **Grace of the Elves** - Charges can give bonus resources
- **Luck of the Dwarves** - Better at receiving rare drops

### Other Boosts
- **Voice of Seren (Trahaearn)** - 20% bonus XP in Prifddinas
- **Urn** - Free 20% bonus XP while training
- **Mining outfit** - 6% bonus XP (5 pieces + 1 bonus)
- **Ancestral pickaxe** - Upgrades pickaxe with bonus effects

## Money Making Methods

### Rune Ore (85+)
- **Profit:** 2-3M GP/hr
- **XP:** 150k-200k XP/hr
- Balance of profit and XP

### Animica (90+)
- **Profit:** Variable
- **XP:** Moderate
- Used for high-level Smithing

### Concentrated Coal (77+)
- **Profit:** 1-2M GP/hr
- **XP:** 100k-150k XP/hr
- Steady income

## Passive Mining Methods

### Mining Guild Resource Dungeon
- AFK with ore box
- Good for afk gains

### Seren Stones
- Best passive method
- Can easily AFK for 5 minutes

## Tips for Efficient Training

1. **Get Mining Urns** - Free 20% bonus XP
2. **Use Perfect Plus Potion** - 5% XP boost + gem finding
3. **Join Trahaearn VoS** - 20% XP boost when active
4. **Complete Mining Outfit** - 6% permanent XP boost
5. **Use Stone Spirits** - Double ores from mining
6. **Augment Your Pickaxe** - Add Honed perk for more XP
7. **Do Daily Challenges** - Free bonus Mining XP
8. **Ore Box** - Hold more ore before banking

## Important Unlocks

### Quest Unlocks
- **Plague's End** (75 Mining req) - Access to Prifddinas & Seren Stones
- **The Dig Site** - Unlocks Exam Centre for fossils
- **Rocking Out** - Better crystal pickaxe

### Achievement Unlocks
- **Mining Outfit** - 6% XP boost (from Stealing Creation or Stealing Creation spotlight)
- **Golden Mining Outfit** - Same as regular + other benefits (from TH or gameplay)

## 1-99 Training Plan

**Levels 1-60:** Mine iron ore and coal at Mining Guild
**Levels 60-99:** AFK at Seren Stones in Prifddinas

**Alternative Fast Route:**
**Levels 1-40:** Iron ore
**Levels 40-60:** Concentrated coal (LRC)
**Levels 60-80:** Seren Stones
**Levels 80-99:** Alaea Crablets (if you want faster XP)

## 99-120 Training

Continue with Seren Stones for AFK or Alaea Crablets for active training.

**XP Required:** 104M total XP for 120
**Time:** 200-400 hours depending on method
