## Quest Categories

### Novice Quests
Beginner-friendly quests with minimal requirements.

### Intermediate Quests
Quests requiring some skill levels and previous quest completion.

### Experienced Quests
Advanced quests with significant skill and quest requirements.

### Master Quests
High-level quest lines with extensive prerequisites.

### Grandmaster Quests
The most challenging quest series in the game.

## Quest Rewards Worth Getting

### Combat
- **The World Wakes** - Combat abilities and XP
- **Ritual of the Mahjarrat** - Access to high-level content
- **The Temple at Senntisten** - Ancient Curses

### Skills
- **Recipe for Disaster** - Barrows gloves (best-in-slot gloves for mid-game)

- **Fate of the Gods** - Divination rewards

### Access
- **Lost City** - Zanaris access

### Transportation
- **Fairy Tale Part II** - Fairy rings
- **The Grand Tree** - Gnome glider network
- **One Small Favour** - Various teleports

## Tips for Questing

1. **Use the Wiki** - The RuneScape Wiki has excellent quick guides
2. **Check Requirements** - Use quest calculators to plan skill training
3. **Do Quest Chains** - Complete series together for better storyline flow
4. **Save Combat Quests** - Some quests give combat XP - save for later levels
5. **Get Quest Cape** - Aim for quest cape for completionist goals
